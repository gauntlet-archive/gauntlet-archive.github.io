Inspired by gothic, young adult, historical, and romantic fantasy (including Ellen Kushner, N.K. Jemisin, V.E. Schwab, Lois McMaster Bujold, Tamora Pierce, and Mercedes Lackey) Barrow Keep uses a restrained fantasy-steampunk aesthetic to create a world of intrigue, treachery, and mystery.

Our game will be anchored by three elements.

  • Barrow Keep by Richard Ruane will provide the setting;
  • Troika! by the Melsonian Arts Council will drive the game mechanics;
  • Dungeons and Dilemmas by Jesse Burneko will supply the adventure architecture.

But all rules will be taught,. No prior experience is necessary, and this will be a beginner-friendly game.
