Durance is a gmless roleplaying game by Jason Morningstar in which players take on the roles of authority and prisoners in a deep space prison colony on a planet that was never meant to support human life. At times, you will be asked to play one of your two notable characters (one from the side of authority and one from the side of the prisoners), and at others, you will be asked to take on the role of guide to the other players, asking provocative questions and pushing conflicts to the razor edge that will cut our throats. Durance is a game about making promises to yourself about what you would never do to survive, and being forced to break them.

This event is scheduled to run for 2 and 1/2 hours. It will be recorded assuming all parties agree.

There will be 3 sessions in total. Being able to attend each session is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct will be in play and safety tools (Script Change and X-Card) will be in use.
