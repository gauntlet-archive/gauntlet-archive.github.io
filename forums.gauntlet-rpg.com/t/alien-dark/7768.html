Alien Dark

In space, no one can hear you scream.

This is a 2-shot of Alun Rees’s Alien Dark. We’ll create characters for a salvage crew and jump right into the action aboard their ship, which has just stumbled across the find of a lifetime….

MarkW January 19, 2022, 6:28pm #2

CONTENT WARNING: Horror, body horror, sci-fi action.

SESSIONS: There will be 2 sessions in total. Being able to attend each session is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

**Please note that the two sessions are two weeks apart instead of the standard one week apart.

DURATION: Each session is 3 hours.

NUMBER OF PLAYERS: Three players in addition to the GM.

SAFETY TOOLS IN USE: X-Card, Lines & Veils, Open Door Policy, Stars & Wishes. The game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

VENUE/TECH: Zoom Video, Online Character Keeper, etc. Expect an email one week before the first session, and another shortly before each session.

BREAKS: Two 5-10 minute breaks at the top of each hour.

Session will be recorded, but would only be posted with full player consent. Currently the plan is to record only for the GM’s reference.

PRIOR EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: None – rules will be taught in play.
