Pasión de las Pasiones: Bajo Cubierta is a send up of the reality tv show Below Deck. You play the captain and overly dramatic young crew of a luxury yacht you’ll be leaving port and heading down to one of the biggest party capitals in the world, Belize ! You’ll be at the beck and call of the wealthiest of the wealthy, you must cater to their every whim. But once you’re off the hook, you can really go off the hook! Between the endless parties, torrid romances and conflicts between crew members with the Captain refereeing the mayhem, and 1% entitlement, this set up is perfect for Pasion de la Pasiones. Even if you’re not a fan of the series it should be ridiculous fun. Come sail away with us to the magical world of Bajo Cubierta!!!
