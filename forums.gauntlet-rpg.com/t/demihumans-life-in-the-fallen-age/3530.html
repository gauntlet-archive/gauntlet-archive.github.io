I’m going to be running my new game for The Gauntlet, and I’d love it if you’d join me. Some details:

  • Demihumans is a hack of Apocalypse World where the apocalypse is the death of everything and everyone magical. Your enclave—a nonhuman community under the control of a human city—is under constant threat, and you play the people who are trying to keep it together and make it thrive. You can see the playbooks, moves, and so on in the play packet.
  • For the first three days, I’d ask nonwhite people only to sign up. Given the subject matter of the game and who I am, it’s important to me to diversify the playtest pool.
  • This will be 4 sessions in total, and run on Tuesdays in November, from 7 pm to 11 pm Eastern time. Sessions will be 3 hours and will not be recorded.
  • You don’t have to attend every session of this series, but it’s appreciated. if you can. RSVP to each event separately.
