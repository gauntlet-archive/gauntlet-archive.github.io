During the days and months after the death of Emperor Palpatine, hope started to return to the galaxy. It grew in fits and starts, like a sapling planted on rocky ground.

In the far reaches of the Outer Rim, a fledging temple grew. The lone Jedi master sent out a group of young padawans in their own ship, with orders to seek out other potential students, others who might be able to understand The Force, who might be tempted by the Dark Side without the proper teaching. And, of course, if the padawans found trouble on those farflung planets, they should seek to bring peace and balance.
