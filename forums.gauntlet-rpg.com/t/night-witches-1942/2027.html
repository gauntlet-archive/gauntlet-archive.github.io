Night Witches: 1942

The Great Patriotic War marches on! The Nazis are figuring out how to respond to your attacks, and this series will see a transition from the harsh but manageable conditions at TRUD GORNYKA to the mud-soaked meat grinder of PASHKOVSKAYA.

This is the third series in a run spanning the entire war! Players may bring characters forward from any previous session, or create a new PC.
