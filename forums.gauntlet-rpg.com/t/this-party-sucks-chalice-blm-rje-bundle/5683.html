Scary Monsters & Nice Sprites

A game of love and intimacy for monsters in a dive at night.

Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites involves all of our characters hanging around at the same, supernaturals only place after dark - a night club, a concert, a bar, a coffeeshop, whatever. For one reason or another, we’re there to unwind, chill, and maybe - just maybe, find somebody to spend the night with.

This game is about hooking up at a club, but doing it within the frame of good fun, mutual intimacy, respect and consent!

Two more games from the Racial Justice & Equality bundle I’m offering soon.

This Party Sucks
This Party Sucks is a collaborative story game about being a queer and/or trans twentysomething who is (badly) processing a recent breakup. You will play out a piece of their story as they attend a series of three parties while trying (and failing) not to think about their ex. The game encourages a group of three players to laugh, wince, and (possibly) reflect on their own lives and relationships while telling their shared protagonist’s story with honesty and tenderness.

Chalice: The Cathedral of Green

Chalice is a collaborative storytelling game about a party of Arthurian knights searching for the Holy Grail.

The game tells the story of the physical and spiritual descent of the knights as they quest for, and ultimately fail to find, the Grail. The knight’s quest is a doomed one of chivalric virtue, undone by their own fatal flaws and moral shortcomings.
