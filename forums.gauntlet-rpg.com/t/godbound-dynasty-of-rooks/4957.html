This is for five sessions of Godbound, a high fantasy game of new divinities played with an old-school system. In this game of exploration and growth you will play emerging gods in the aftermath of a cosmic war, just coming into your powers. In session one we will set tone and safety, make characters, establish facts about our world, and get playing.

Rules will be taught (familiarity with retroclone and OSR games will be helpful). Attendance at all sessions is strong encouraged. As of the posting of this, Godbound can be found in the Bundle of Holding.

“Godbound is a game of divine heroes in a broken world, of men and women who have seized the tools that have slipped from an absent God’s hands. Bound to the Words of Creation, these new-forged titans face a world ravaged by madmen and fools. Their foes are many: jealous parasite gods, a furious Angelic Host that once held Heaven against the armies of men, and endless legions of sorcerers, god-monsters, mortal tyrants, and eldritch relicts. The Godbound stand against these horrors, determined to forge a better world from the fragments of the old.“

We will use Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines & Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
