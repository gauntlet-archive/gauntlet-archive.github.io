I think your intuitions on this are likely close to the reason. I think these games are designed to be played over a largish number of sessions. That gives you time to get comfortable with how spending the currency will impact your play in the short and long term. Uncertainty about that breeds hoarding.

Another piece of it though is that a lot of these systems are designed to get players to do things they might not always feel comfortable doing (ie PvP or causing drama). If your players are already comfortable with doing that stuff they are just going to do it and won‚Äôt really need to make use of the economy per se.

I might be wrong, but I don‚Äôt think any of these systems have a penalty for not making use of the currency. And if you have the situation where the players do the thing without needing to use the currency then just saying ‚Äúyou lose it all at the end of the session‚Äù might not be enough.
