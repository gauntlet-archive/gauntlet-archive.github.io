I’m running The Bat Hack for Gauntlet Comics! It’s my version of The Black Hack RPG for minor league baseball players. The PCs will all play for the Gauntlet City Goblins. If we have new players, we’ll create characters, and regardless of rookie status or not, we’ll learn about why they dream of making it to the big leagues, play through a couple of games, and see what their lives are like off the field, too.

(A seat in this series is reserved for Jesse Abelman)

This game is part of the ongoing Gauntlet Comics series. Characters and settings created in this miniseries will exist in the Gauntlet Comics canon (and on our wiki) for future miniseries.

Being able to attend every session is encouraged, but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct will be in play and safety tools Lines and Veils and X-Card) will be in use.
