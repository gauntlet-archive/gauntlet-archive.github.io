It’s 3AM. There’s someone in your house. They’re going to kill you.

Quietus is a tabletop roleplaying game of desperate and melancholy horror about ordinary people struggling against an implacable force. You’ll create characters with tragic backstories, and send them hurtling headlong into a nightmare where their past trauma might be the only thing that saves them.

This is a one shot game of Quietus, my Forged in the Dark game of melancholy horror, which will be coming to Kickstarter this June. Sessions will be anywhere between an hour and a half to two and a half hours and presuming everyone agrees, will be recorded.

This is an emotional and often very dark game, the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and safety tools (open door policy and X-Card) will be in use.

Each session is a one shot, so you only need to respond to one session.
