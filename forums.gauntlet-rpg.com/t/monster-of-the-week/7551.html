Monster of the Week

Most people don’t believe in monsters, but you know the truth. They’re real, and it’s your task to bring them down. Inspired by media such as Buffy, Supernatural, The X-Files, and Fringe, create a team of bad-ass Hunters and face the horrors that stalk the land.

In this five-session run we’ll play one or more of the scenarios from the Tome of Mysteries expansion.

NOTE: This is my first true outing with PBTA, so please only sign up if you’re ready to deal with a facilitator finding his feet. Cheers.

CONTENT WARNING: Violence, horror, the supernatural, possibly body horror, romance.

SESSIONS: 5

DURATION: 3 hours.

NUMBER OF PLAYERS: Three players in addition to the facilitator.

SAFETY TOOLS IN USE: X-Card, Lines & Veils, Open Door Policy, Stars & Wishes. The game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

VENUE/TECH: Zoom Video, Roll20 Character Sheets. Expect an email one week before the first session with the Roll20 link, and another shortly before the session.

BREAKS: Two 5-10 minute breaks at the top of each hour.

RECORDING: Sessions will be recorded, but would only be posted with full enthusiastic player consent. Currently the plan is to record only for the facilitator’s reference.

PRIOR EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: None – rules will be taught in play.
