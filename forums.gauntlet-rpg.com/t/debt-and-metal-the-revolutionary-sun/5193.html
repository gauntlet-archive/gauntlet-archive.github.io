Debt and Metal: the Revolutionary Sun

The sessions will be recorded, uploaded publicly to YouTube and converted into an audio-only format; by signing up for a session, you agree to be recorded and for the recording to be used in this way.

You’ve been stuck on this asteroid depot — aces, freighters, caravans, and everything in between stopping for fuel and that’s it — for who knows how long. Long enough for the bartender to know your name and the bounty agent to wish they didn’t. You and your squad are bored and the opportunity of a lifetime’s about to drop in your lap. Maybe you’ll be thankful for it. Maybe it’ll get you off of this rock, at least. It doesn’t matter really: you’re an ace and that’s what aces do. They take the contract too gray for any self-respecting nation-state to vet and usually too difficult for any run-of-the-mill private security. At least you’ll be able to finally get a good drink, that much is for sure.

These are four sessions of Debt and Metal by Darren Brockes, a game of mecha and mercenaries Rooted in Trophy. This is a playtest. Being able to attend each session is ideal, but not required. You must sign up for each session you wish to attend.

We’ll use Zoom and Google Sheets. I’ll email all players about a week before the session with necessary information and links.

Content Warnings: violent conflict, psychosomatic harm, capitalism
Safety Tools: Script Change, Lines and Veils, the Community Code of Conduct
