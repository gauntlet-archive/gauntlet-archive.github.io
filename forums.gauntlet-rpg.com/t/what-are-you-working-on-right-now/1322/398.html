Gus.L January 22, 2022, 7:19pm #404

Currently finishing the draft of an adventure for Ava Islam’s Errant called CURSE OF THE GANSHOGGR. It’s about helping or hindering the warlike and vain Goose King (aka “Sigtrygg II, Gosvater, Sea King of the Anser”) avoid divine punishment for his misrule by slaying a ‘dragon’ - the Ganshoggr. As an adventure it’s largely my effort to write a somewhat gonzo (it’s a Kingdom of Geese modeled a bit after Viking age Dublin) fairy tale and also an experiment in wrapping a whole low level adventure around a single extremely dangerous creature that they players must figure out how to defeat (or aid, or just plunder from). Mansfeld March 4, 2022, 1:52pm #409

I’m working on two things, at the moment.

  • The game for single player, about loss of your own neurodivergent identity for purpose of "being normal". Essentially “what if those pseudo-science about curing autism would be true”. This project doesn’t have a title yet. It will follow PbtA philosophy (essentially, “Ironsworn meets Glitch”.
  • The second add-on for my game October Rust, as a collection of optional rules, additional backgrounds, trapping and so on. The title will be “Suffering from Success”. The original game itself is about personal fall for greater stand, or - more crudely saying - for your last heist against ungrateful world (early modern fantasy).
