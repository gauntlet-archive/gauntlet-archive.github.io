The Farrier's Bellows is here!

Announcements
jasoncordova February 27, 2019, 4:22pm #1

We are launching a brand-new podcast today! The Farrier’s Bellows is all about story games, and the first episode focuses on the Forge-era classic, InSpectres! The show was designed to be a sister podcast to Fear of a Black Dragon, and so you’ll notice some similarities between their formats.

THE GAUNTLET

InSpectres

The Farrier's Bellows is a production of Zack W., Diana, and Zack B. 

As usual, it takes a few days for the podcast to show up on iTunes and Google Play. We’ll update this post with links once we have them. In the meantime, you can listen to the show on our site (linked above) or find it in your podcatcher app by using this URL (be sure to enter it precisely as you see it):

http://farriersbellows.libsyn.com/rss

Enjoy the show, and let us know what you think!
