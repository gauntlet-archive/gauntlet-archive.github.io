Upon the White-Fire Hill stands the derelict Castle Rex. Once the seat of The Creator, a powerful sorcerer who bent reality and the local population to his every whim, it now stands vacant and crumbling after the Creator’s death and the ensuing wars. Now foolhardy and desperate treasure hunters seek their fortunes and their chance at fame and glory in the depths of the castle, seeking out the font of The Creator’s power. Will you be the ones to harness the Soul Sword Forge?

Trophy Gold is a game of desperate adventurers; treasure hunters who must brave the depths of the world to recover treasure and pay their debts. We will be running through the module The Soul Sword Forge.

This is a 4 session series. Attendance at all four sessions is highly recommended but not required.

You can find more information about Trophy Gold at https://trophyrpg.com/. The rules of the game will be taught, no prior knowledge is necessary.

This game will take place using Role. It will be recorded and shared with player permission.

Content Warnings and Safety Tools

This module (and Trophy as a system) will likely touch on the following topics. We can use Lines and Veils to mitigate some of these if necessary, but by default these will be in play:

  • Graphic descriptions of injury or violence
  • Classism and oppression
  • Desperation, obsession, and poverty

We will be using Lines and Veils, Script Change, the X card, and the Open Door policy. All of these will be explained at the beginning of the session. This game follows The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
