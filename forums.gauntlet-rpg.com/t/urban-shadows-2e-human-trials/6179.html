Checking out Urban Shadows 2e! A seat is being held for Fraser Simons, so three players total (and me MCing).

urban-fantasy-featured-image960×439 125 KB

A grisly scene has been found in one of the biology labs of the University, but no one can figure it out: a leyline correction? A sacrificial ritual? Just a plain, old murder? While the mundane world hasn’t quite heard about it yet, the supernatural world is scrambling to point fingers and return to the fragile truces they had–but it’s different now. In the middle of winter no less, something has to be done.

Content Warnings: Physical violence, social manipulation, werewolves (body transformations), vampires (consent, blood)
System: Urban Shadows 2e quickstart
Breaks: Two ten minute breaks after one and two hours.
Tools: Role
Recording: I would like to record the sessions, but will only do so with everyone’s explicit consent (which can be withdrawn later).
Safety: This game adheres to the Community Code of Conduct. Lines & Veils and Script Change will be in use, along with an open door policy.
