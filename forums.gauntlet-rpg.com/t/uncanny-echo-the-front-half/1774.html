Uncanny Echo (The Front Half)

Gauntlet Calendar
JimLikesGames April 16, 2019, 8:58pm #1

There’s always been something not quite right about this city. Things you see out of the corner of your eye, conversations just out of earshot, a smell you can’t place but that you swear you remember.

Uncanny Echo is a prestige TV series of modern urban weirdness. Think The Wire if it were written by Rod Serling. We’ll play to find out what’s so strange about this place, in a series of one-shots that will tie together in ways we haven’t figured out yet.

Sign up for one or more, as you wish, each is a self-contained one-shot that happens in the same setting over time, but aren’t directly tied by a tight continuity. The entire series runs 10 episodes, with the back half planned for July.

CW: Modern hard-boiled strangeness. Prepare to play a range of genres from crime to edgy sitcom, with a range of characters. Presume sex, drugs, and violence at a premium streaming drama level.

X-Card will be in effect, with Open Door Policy and Lines and Veils . Sessions will be recorded but only posted with affirmative consent from all participants at the end of the session.
