Opportunities to Play! Games with Open Seats Available 2019/04/01 thru 2019/04/06

Gauntlet Calendar
need_players
shane April 1, 2019, 11:15am #1

Hey folks!

In this post, we will highlight games for the upcoming week which have open seats and therefore the opportunity to add some more gaming fun to your week.

Slings & Arrows

Tuesday, April 2, 2019 10:00 PM
Slings & Arrows is about theatre people and how the show they’re in seeps into their day jobs and personal lives.

The Crimson Shadow of a Golden Cross playtest

Tuesday, April 2, 2019 11:00 PM
This game is set in an original setting based on the Gothic Horror genre. It is meant to tell stories of religious corruption, class struggle, cultural oppression and grey morality.

Lacuna Part 1: The Creation of the Mystery and The Girl from Blue City

Tuesday, April 2, 2019 11:00 PM
Lacuna Part 1. The Creation of the Mystery and the Girl from Blue City is an experimental roleplaying game . . . and you’re part of the experiment whether you realize it or not.

Gauntlet Comics: Homefront Heroes (Masks)

Wednesday, April 3, 2019 12:00 AM
We’ll turn back the clock to the heady days of War Bonds and Ration Cards to portray the teen heroes tasked with defending Gauntlet City against saboteurs, fifth columnists, and the sinister BLitzKinder!

Afterlife Wandering Souls: The Wall of Thorns

Wednesday, April 3, 2019 6:00 PM
Afterlife: Wandering Souls is a strange fantasy game where you play a Wanderer—a traveler who passed from the world of the living into the world of the dead. With no memories of who you once were your goal is to discover truths about yourself—as hard as that may be.

Dungeon World: The Slave-Pit of Drazhu

Thursday, April 4, 2019 3:00 AM
You’ve been enslaved by the evil lich Drazhu and his vicious minions, who have put you to work digging tunnels to expand Drazhu’s underground lair. Life sucks bad! What do you do?

The Between: Beta Playtest

Thursday, April 4, 2019 6:00 PM
If you like Penny Dreadful and PbtA: PLAY THIS GAME!

Mecha Monthly: Armour Astir

Thursday, April 4, 2019 6:00 PM
A high-fantasy take on mecha warfare, play as rebellious magicians, fighters, clerics, and forty-foot tall magical constructs called Astirs, caught up in the struggle against an Authority that seeks to control them.

Meadowsea - Heroes: Fellowship

Saturday, April 6, 2019 3:00 PM
This game is part of the ongoing Meadowsea series, a collection of tales inspired by the Redwall series of books starring brave little woodland critters set in an idyllic prairie landscape. Fellowship is a tabletop adventure game where you work together to save the world from an evil overlord bent on destroying everything. The game is built around the adventure - traveling the world, facing down challenges, protecting the weak, and defeating the grand villain attempting to destroy everything your characters love.

Monsterhearts 2: The Institute

Saturday, April 6, 2019 6:00 PM
We are going to explore the monster-for-queerness aspect of Monsterhearts in a school that is much like the X-Men Academy or Hogwarts. The prom is coming up and you need a date to be super cool like all the awesome monsters in the school!
