Cthulhu Deep Green: Control Group are two self-contained scenarios meant as recruiting missions for the Delta Green conspiracy. Player characters will be unaware of the super-natural and ill-equipped to deal with the situation at hand. The lucky few who survive will have proven themselves to be trustworthy of confronting unnatural threats and keeping them secret.

Cthulhu Deep Green is a horror roleplaying game and will include violence and gore. Please check if the individual themes align with your preferences. We will be using the X-Card, CATS and other safety tools that players suggest. The game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct. We will be using streamyard. The session will be recorded but only made public if all players agree. I will send a reminder per email a week before we play.

This is part of the Delta Green shared world. Although we will be using a modified version of Cthulhu Dark, characters from this series will be converted to the normal Delta Green rules at the end of the session.
