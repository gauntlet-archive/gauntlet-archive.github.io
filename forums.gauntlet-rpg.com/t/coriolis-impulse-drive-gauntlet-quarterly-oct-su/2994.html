This is for the first of three October sessions of Coriolis: Impulse Drive. We will be using the ID PbtA system to play out stories in the Coriolis universe. This campaign will run throughout Q4 2019 (Oct-Dec). Attendance at all sessions is encouraged, but not required. Rules will be taught. In session one we will discuss tone, create characters, and get our story started.

Sessions will be on Oct 6th, 20th, and 27th. Note that the 27th session will be during Gauntlet Con.

Coriolis is a science fiction role playing (setting of) a remote cluster of star systems called The Third Horizon. It is a place ravaged by conflicts and war, but also home to proud civilizations, both new and old. In this game, you will crew a space ship and travel the Horizon.

You will explore the ancient ruins of the Portal Builders, undertake missions for the powerful factions and partake in the game of political intrigue on Coriolis station - the centre of power in the Third Horizon. You might even encounter strange beings from the dark between the stars.

From the Monolith in the jungles of Kua to the floating temples of Mira, the Horizon is yours to explore. In the end you might even discover the truth about the mysterious Emissaries and the threat of the dark between the stars.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and the X-Card safety tool will be in use.
