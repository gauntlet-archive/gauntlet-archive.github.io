Your family has been looking to add a feline friend. You’ve visited the local animal shelter and spent some time with a cat who just might be perfect – or just might be a disaster. It’s time to reflect on your experiences with this cat, and pets in general, before you decide to make the big commitment.

Forever Home is a Descended From The Queen game of animal companions and the people who love (and hate) them.

This session will run for three hours, and will be recorded and published with the consent of all participants. The game will be held via Streamyard.

Lines and veils, the X card, and the Gauntlet’s inclusivity policy will be in effect.
