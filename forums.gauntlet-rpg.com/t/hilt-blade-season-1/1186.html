Here are four episodes scheduled for the first season of HILT // BLADE! The first episode will be four hours long and the remaining three.
The episodes will be streamed on YouTube and recorded for Gauntlet Hangouts. You will also need to record your audio with a separate program (like QuickTime on Mac or Voice Recorder on Windows) to upload after each session.
Being able to attend each session is ideal, but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

You are part of a paramilitary organization that is solely responsible for combatting IMMINENT THREATS, gigantic monstrosities that necessitated the creation of BLADES — giant, piloted robot-weapons. You are part of a squad with the other teenage HILTS, one squad of many, and overseen by a DOCENT, your only face-to-face contact within the paramilitary organization. The DOCENT is responsible for your well-being and training and will always push you to complete your mission — you are, after all, just an asset to the organization. Just like your BLADE.

We will use the X-card and Script Change during our sessions. We will observe the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy.
