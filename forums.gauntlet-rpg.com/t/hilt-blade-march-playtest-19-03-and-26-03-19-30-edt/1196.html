I have room for tonight’s session of HILT // BLADE, and if you can make this session and the next, I can squeeze you in for the March 26 one!

HILT // BLADE is a game being developed by Darren Brockes. It tells the story of dysfunctional teenagers trying to live their lives while protecting everyone else’s — a duty they never chose.

We’ll be dealing with the intense emotional spectrum of teenage lives combined with the pressure and danger of large-scale fights for survival of humankind.

The X-card will be used, and we will be observing the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy (http://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html).
