You are squires on the cusp of knighthood, venturing into the treacherous Bleakwood on a quest to prove yourselves worthy and discover the elusive Tower Perilous. What wrongs will you right and lessons will you learn along the way?

Vow of the Knight-Aspirants is a PbtA game of fantasy adventure, inspired by Arthurian legends, fairy tales, and Magic: The Gathering 's plane of Eldraine. This will be its very first playtest. In this playtest, we’ll build characters and throw them into some action, hopefully testing out the game’s modular playbooks, basic moves, and the Lesson mechanic that can grant Advantage or Disadvantage.

DURATION: 3 and 1/3 hours

NUMBER OF PLAYERS: 4 players total.

SAFETY TOOLS IN USE: We will use CATS, the X-Card, Lines & Veils, and the Open Door Policy. This event abides by the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

SET UP INFO: We will play over Streamyard. Expect a set-up email from me about a week before the session, with a link to a Google sheet character keeper.

BREAKS: two ten-minute breaks (and more as requested)

RECORDED: Provided all players give permission, sessions will be recorded for my reference, and possibly posted to the Cloven Pine Games Youtube channel.

PRIOR EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: No, rules will be taught in play.

Vow of the Knight-Aspirants: Into the Bleakwood
Starring: Brandon B, Marc M, @GroupSoliloquy, David L
In the very first playtest of a new game, we follow a quartet of squires venturing into a dark fairy tale wood to prove themselves worthy of knighthood. Bronwen strikes down a wolf that turns out to be a cursed man. Lancer recovers tome full of spells and considers whom it rightly belongs to. Ozadeam glimpses a terrible future and works to prevent it. Caelia taunts an angry mob and a bitter sorceress to buy time and space for her allies.
