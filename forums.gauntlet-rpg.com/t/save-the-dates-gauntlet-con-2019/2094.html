Save the Dates: Gauntlet Con 2019!

Announcements

RichardRuane May 23, 2019, 8:10pm #1

Gauntlet Con is back this October, so save the dates!

The first games will start at Thursday, October 24, 2019 6:00 PM and the last games will wrap up by Monday, October 28, 2019 3:00 AM. Gauntlet Con is an entirely online con with participants in many timezones and countries. The con organizing team is currently working on panels, workshops, guests, prizes, and – of course – many, many games.

If you have questions about Gauntlet Con, please let me know! If you would like to discuss guests and guest invitations for Gauntlet Con, please reach out to me privately.
