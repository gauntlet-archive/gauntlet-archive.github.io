This is a Neitherworld Event!
Set in an alternate Earth where New Gauntlet City exists in North Mexico. The characters from previous sessions of Gauntlet Comics might appear here like objects in a mirror, slightly different but even more interesting.

Nahual is a tabletop roleplaying game of Mexican urban fantasy. In Nahual, you play as shapeshifting nahuales who hunt angels to make a living. You run a changarro—a business—together and sell the angels you hunt as different types of products. Caught in the middle of an ancient feud, you struggle to find your place in a world of fantastical and overwhelming forces.

Nahual builds upon the Powered by the Apocalypse system used in Apocalypse World, Urban Shadows, Dungeon World and more. It is set in the universe created by Mexican comic book artist Edgar Clément. The game is written by Miguel Angel Espinoza.

This game is part of the ongoing Gauntlet Comics series. Characters and settings created in this miniseries will exist in the Gauntlet Comics canon (and on our wiki) for future miniseries.

Being able to attend every session is not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

The X-Card safety tool will be on the table. For more information on the Gauntlet’s safety tools, please refer to our Inclusivity Policy on the website (http://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html)
