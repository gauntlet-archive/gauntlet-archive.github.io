Welcome to Gauntlet Comics and it’s time for an imprint of Masked Mustelid Comics! We here at Masked Mustelid like exploring the realms of comics that are cutting edge and show different perspectives. This iteration will focus on villains of New Gauntlet City in the modern age who have been training at the School of Hard Knocks aka SOHK (pronounced sock, like the old Westian sound effect), a prestigious program that has been designed to perfect the practice of villainy. This is not a place for selfish actions or leaving your teammates behind for heroes to capture. By learning to act in concert with other villains you should be able to take on heroes years your senior and stand up to those upstart youthful brats who constantly pick on you.

You are in one of the first graduating classes of the School of Hard Knocks, pieced together because you help cover up deficiencies in other teammates. With the files the schools has on the heroes of the past mixed with your prowess the world is open for you to carve a niche for yourself in the city of super heroes.

This game is heavily based on Villains United, The Secret Six, and the Thunderbolts style of comics. I want to have fun playing villains and humanize them rather than demonize heroes. If you’ve always wanted to rename the Brotherhood of Evil Mutants because their goals didn’t seem that bad then this is the game for you.
