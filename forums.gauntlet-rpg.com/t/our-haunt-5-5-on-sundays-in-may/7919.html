A broken down mansion, a few shattered memories, a whispered song, the promise of a family, and the watchful eyes of something sinister.

Welcome home

Our Haunt is a gm-full story-telling game by Rae Nedjadi about ghosts haunting a mansion, about finding out who they were and who they are now, and what other things lurk in their home. This is the first of five sessions.

This game will have two guests: Curious Cat and Rae Nedjadi.

System : Our Haunt

Duration : Five 3 hour sessions

Breaks : 1 on top of every hour

Venue : GM’s Zoom

Recording : No
