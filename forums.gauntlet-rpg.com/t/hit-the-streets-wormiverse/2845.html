Hit the Streets: Wormiverse

Luiz August 12, 2019, 11:32pm #1

This is is a four-session series. Attending all sessions is fun but not mandatory. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

Hit the Streets: Defend the Block is a system built to tell stories of street-level super-powered people who are trying to deal with the struggle of everyday life and the responsibilities of having superpowers and a conscience. The characters we’ll create won’t be heroes, but they will still stand against the threats that plague their neighborhood. Or at least try to.

We will be playing in the setting of Worm, a gritty super-hero web serial by John McCrae. Knowledge of the setting is a major plus, but you can still join if you are not familiar with it; I will ask you to read a brief doc detailing the peculiarities of super-powers in it though. We will play in the period soon after the Echdina crisis, where the existence of Cauldron capes is revealed to the public.

The way super-powers are handled in the wormiverse makes it so trauma is central to the life of most supers, so we will most likely touch on some heavy themes. This is not a light-hearted super-hero romp.

The X-card will be used, and we will be observing the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy.
