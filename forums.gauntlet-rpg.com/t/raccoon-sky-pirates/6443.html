Take to the skies! Raccoon Sky Pirates is a short, GM-less story game for 3â€“6 players. In it, you play a scruffy, ring-tailed agent of chaos. Find ingenious uses for trash and embrace joyful, random action.

Your home is in the junkyard. There, you and your crew built a ship out of a wrecked car, a home appliance, an old carnival ride, and other junk. You fly it to the suburbs, striving to keep it together for the sake of your shipmates. Descend on a suburban house. Steal all the Crate-and-Barrel furniture and smart-home gadgets you can find. This game takes the stuffing out of materialist, capitalist suburban life, then invites you to dive into the hole you made by taking that stuffing out. Just inside those double-paned windows is enough loot to make you pirate royalty.
