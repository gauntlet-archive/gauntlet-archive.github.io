World Wide Wrestling 2nd edition: Pro Wrestling Nova

Gauntlet Calendar

Come join us in Gauntlet City as the newest Wrestling promotion launches it’s first Show.

Having searched the independent scene for the hottest talent, and taking on several stars recently released by the major promotions, this new promotion must find it’s way in the formative days.

Will they expand their fanbase? will they keep their TV deal? Will they survive the ego of those who work there?

Find out on Sunday nights on Pro Wrestling Nova!

We will be playing the Playtest edition of World Wide Wrestling 2nd edition.

The game will run at 8pm GMT and will run for 3 hours.

Lines and Veils and the X-Card will be used as safety rules.
