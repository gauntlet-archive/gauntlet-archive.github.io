linfin March 3, 2020, 5:25pm #59


Trophaeum is a series of classics-inspired adventures for use with Trophy Dark, or any OSR system.

The Zine:

Trophaeum will be about 24 pages, single color, saddle-stitched, at the standard half-page zine size. It will be published through Risolve Studio, an ethically sourced and sustainable printing press that uses soy-based ink. It will include the following:

  • Pictland: You play as centurions, sent north of Hadrian’s Wall to find the lost eagle of the Ninth Century, Hispania. Picts and the highland forest meet you at every turn, drawing you deeper and deeper into the heath and heather, hungry for red Roman Blood.
  • Out of Crete: You are the second group of unwilling youth Minos sacrifices to the Minotaur, seven years after the first band left Athens. Deep in Daedalus’ labyrinth, who will survive? Can you escape the bull and flee the island?
  • An expanded list of rituals, occupations, and backgrounds
  • 36 Reminders of Rome: Rome weighs heavy on the minds of all who leave her. Whether Rome is an ideal, a conqueror, or one’s home, it’s hard to escape the lure of the Empire.
  • 36 Deities to Whom You Pray: Your home has been Rome, but perhaps not always. There are gods that walk in your hearth, some that have been passed down womb to tomb, the gods of bootstraps and bright swords, the gods of the glacier, the gods of bone.
  • Six interior illustrations.
