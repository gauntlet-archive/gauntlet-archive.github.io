There used to be a school for you, but AEON trashed it a couple of years ago. Now no one cares about you- not your parents, not the teachers, and not the baselines who swarm these halls. You’re the villains. Well, not really the villains but you’re the kids of villains, children of henches, running away from the first time your powers went off and killed someone, or just so far down the food chain that the capes and heroes flying through the sky will never even notice you.

This is for sessions of Monsterhearts 2 set in Gauntlet Comics. We will lightly reskin the playbooks to fit these characters: teens with powers and a history which drags them down. They attend a conventional high school, trying to avoid notice and find themselves. Attendance at all sessions is strongly encouraged. Rules will be taught. In session one we will make characters, talk about the tone we want—the PG13 melodrama of The CW or the grittier R-rated version from Showtime.

Content Warning: These sessions may contain problematic material based on teen lives. We will talk about those limits, establish lines & veils, and go over our safety tools each session.

Sessions run 2 ½ to 3 hours. We will record the session for later posting and use the X-Card for safety. If you sign up and your attendance or RSVP changes, please also send me a message at edige23 at gmail. I will send out a group email a week before the session with set up and invite details.
