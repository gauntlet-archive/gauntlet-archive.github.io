Two series that I keep returning to and wish I could find gamers who really wanted to dig into them:

  • The Vorkosigan series by Lois McMaster Bujold. Bujold writes difficult/unlikely/emotionally and socially broken protagonists that I wish were easier to create in action-oriented games without point-buy driven merits and flaws. The novels are space opera, driven by the need to solve difficult, explosive situations without resorting to violent measures, and I‚Äôd love to see that sort of thinking in more trad-style games.

Violence is dangerous (though frequently not deadly it does end careers and start lifelong political grudges), and while magic is almost unheard of in the setting, it is strange and extremely powerful when it does rear its head.
