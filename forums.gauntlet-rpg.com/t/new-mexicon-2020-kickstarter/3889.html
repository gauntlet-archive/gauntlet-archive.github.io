New MexiCon 2020 Kickstarter

New MexiCon is a small RPG convention in Albuquerque, New Mexico. We focus exclusively on indie and small press games, the kinds of things that don’t receive much attention at other venues.

We just launched the kickstarter to pre-sell badges. If you’re interested in these kinds of games and are in the southwest US, I hope you give it a look and consider joining us in April!

We have backing tiers for a badge, a badge and a hotel room, and just a support tier if you can’t attend but want to help us out.
