desert is a GMless Cyberpunk roleplaying game set in a utopian city where ubiquitous AI helps you work, create, relax, connect, care, be safe. In desert, it’s their randomly generated surroundings that take an interest in the characters, eschewing conflict and catastrophe in favour of small interactions. Have AI offer to +enhance your look as you step into a mirrored elevator on your way to work. Get +involve-d by fixing a damaged municipal microgarden nearby. Let AI +introduce you to the local balloon club.

The full playtest rules are available in pdf here: https://tearlessretina.blogspot.com/2020/08/desert-v122-cyberpunk-roleplaying-game.html

This session is scheduled for 3 hours. It will not be recorded.
