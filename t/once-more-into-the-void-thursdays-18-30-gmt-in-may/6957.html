Once, you were a crew of smugglers, ne’er-do-wells, scoundrels with a heart of gold, getting into and out of scrapes with the heavy-handed authorities. Maybe you started a revolution. Maybe you caused an the Grand Reptile’s empire to fall. You made some money, that’s for sure, and got a few scars, some more sexy than others.

Then you split up, maybe you just wanted peace, or your own island, or you were a functionary in the newly-minted alliance for freedom or something like that. Maybe you were just fed up with everyone’s bullshit. You’ve avoided each other ever since, so it’s probably the last one.

But now your old Captain is back with a final mission. Your old enemy, the Grand Reptile, appears to be back, and they seem to be stronger - and richer - than ever. You need to confront them, take them down, or finally figure out what it was all about. Are you with the Captain?

This is a game of Once More Into the Void, by Jamile Nedjadi. It’s been financed on Kickstarter recently, but a playable version is already out, so we’ll use this (unless a newer version appears,) We’ll aim for three session so we’ll have enough time to develop the characters and the setting - it’s possible that we finish in two sessions, but we’ll talk about that if & when it happens.

The default setting for Once More Into the Void is a science-fiction-setting, so we’ll go with that. Everything else we’ll just make up during the session. It’s a gm-ful game, so I’ll facilitate and guide, but I’ll play a character as well.

Kalum will be a guest star for these games.

Being able to attend all sessions is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

No experience is needed; everything will be taught in the game.

Venue : Zoom

Contact : Players will receive an e-mail to check in about a week before the start of the game.

We will discuss Content, Aim, Tone, and Safety Tools in the first sessions. We will use Lines & Veils, the X-Card, and the Open Door policy, and I will prepare a character keeper as a shared document.

The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in effect for this gam e (https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/community-code-of-conduct.html)!
