Hey folks!

In this post, we will highlight games for the upcoming week which have open seats and therefore the opportunity to add some more gaming fun to your week.

DW Mondays: The Sky Chain

Monday, April 22, 2019 11:30 PM → Tuesday, April 23, 2019 2:30 AM
Islands floating in the sky? How strange, is it natural? Is it magical? Why are there giant chains anchoring these floating islands to the ground? Would they just float away if the chain was broken? Has anyone ever made it to the highest island? The stories say the islands have been up there longer than anyone alive can remember, even the elves, but there are tales that say the islands weren’t always up there. Perhaps those of you who are brave enough should climb up the chain.

Get Ready 2 Rock (Playtest)

Tuesday, April 23, 2019 12:00 AM → Tuesday, April 23, 2019 3:00 AM
Your band has a big gig coming up, and you’ll need to keep your rockstar antics in check if you want to keep getting more shows. But on the other hand, what’s the point of being in a band if you can’t live on the wild side? Make a date with stardom in this GM-less, Powered by the Apocalypse game of musical mayhem.

Never Tell Me the Odds: Ol’ Hallelujah

Tuesday, April 23, 2019 12:00 AM → Tuesday, April 23, 2019 3:00 AM
YOU ARE A SPACEFARING SCOUNDREL. You’ve blasted, cheated, swindled, double-crossed, charmed, and hyper- jumped your way across HALF THE GALAXY. But now, you’ve made a few bad decisions and gotten into some REAL TROUBLE. The one thing in all the universe that you really care about is in terrible danger, and keeping it safe might require the ULTIMATE SACRIFICE. Are you desperate enough to risk everything you have to SAVE WHAT MATTERS MOST?

Lacuna Part 1: The Creation of the Mystery and The Girl from Blue City

Tuesday, April 23, 2019 11:00 PM → Wednesday, April 24, 2019 2:00 AM
Is it a dream? Is it a nightmare? Or is it just a game? And are you already playing? Lacuna Part 1. The Creation of the Mystery and the Girl from Blue City is an experimental roleplaying game . . . and you’re part of the experiment whether you realize it or not.

World Wide Wrestling: Gage Valley Wrestling: Parade of Heroes

Tuesday, April 23, 2019 7:00 PM
OFFICE OF PUBLIC INFORMATION BROADCAST

REJOICE, loyal citizens of Gauntletia. Our Supreme and Beloved leader is pleased to announce that on the night of the 23rd of April, in celebration of his 40th birthday he has commanded a performance that is truly unique.

As a sign of the GREAT RESPECT that the imperialist americans hold our Supreme and beloved leader in, they will send their greatest warriors to fight for the honour of being declared the Greatest fighter in the world. On this night of nights they will battle to win his favour and demonstrate their skills before the gathered masses of the Citizens of our Great and Bounteous motherland of Gauntletia.

Attendance is mandatory.

Afterlife Wandering Souls: The Drowned Lands

Wednesday, April 24, 2019 6:00 PM → Thursday, April 18, 2019 10:00 PM
Afterlife: Wandering Souls is a strange fantasy game where you play a Wanderer—a traveler who passed from the world of the living into the world of the dead. With no memories of who you once were your goal is to discover truths about yourself—as hard as that may be.

Dungeon World: Beyond the Slave-Pit

Thursday, April 25, 2019 3:00 AM
What happens to the survivors of the Slave-Pit of Drazhu? Stay tuned as we flesh out the survivor’s back story and we play to find out what they do next!

Mecha Monthly: Armour Astir

Thursday, April 25, 2019 6:00 PM → Thursday, April 25, 2019 9:00 PM
A high-fantasy take on mecha warfare, play as rebellious magicians, fighters, clerics, and forty-foot tall magical constructs called Astirs, caught up in the struggle against an Authority that seeks to control them.

Storm Riders: GRUNTS

Friday, April 26, 2019 12:00 AM
When Freedom is threatened, when Liberty is under siege, when the forces of evil are knocking on your door, that’s when the GRUNTS (General Reserve United Nations Tactical Squad) mobilize! A Gauntlet Comics presentation! We’ll use @Michael_G_Barford’s Storm Riders to recreate the adventures of Solomon Toys’ massively popular 80s military action figures and the associated mega-hit afternoon cartoon (Look to GI Joe, MASK, and Green Plastic Army Men for inspiration).

Gauntlet Games Now

Sunday, April 28, 2019 9:00 PM
Gauntlet Games Now is a our ‘on demand’ gaming event. There are multiple slots. Since we are setting it up to be “on demand”. Game groups will be created based on who shows up to each slot. We’ll be using the registration system to get an idea of how many facilitators we’ll need for each slot. So please sign up and get on the waitlist. I will be looking at the waitlist and trying to recruit more game facilitators if there is enough demand.
