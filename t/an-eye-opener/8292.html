Hi, Ezra Rose’s zine on cultural appropriation of the Kabbalah was a (third ) eye opener for me:

itch.io

FYMA: A Lesser Key by Ezra Rose

academic zine on appropriation & antisemitism in western occult movements


I like messing with myths and stuff, and it sometimes makes me miss what it can also mean for other people. Believers, alright, that’s one thing ; but also white supremacists, and that’s a whole different thing. Maybe it’s totally clear in your cultural background, it certainly wasn’t in mine.
So if you want to know more, I strongly suggest you read the zine and maybe drop a token of appreciation in the authrix’s* hat.

*(yes, author is a masculine, as in actor or emperor)
