This is for five Tuesday November sessions of Tachyon Squadron, a game of space fighter pilots using Fate. We will be playing with stunts and modifications to fit the setting and premise. Rules will be taught. Attendance at all sessions is strongly encouraged. In session one we will talk safety, make up characters, establish relationships, and get to playing.

General RSVPs open 9/25

The Pitch: The universe is in trouble. Take your stand against the overreaching arms of the Dominion of Unity. Stand up against piracy and lawlessness. Ensure that all members of the Draconis system enjoy peace and prosperity. Join the Tachyon Squadron arm of the Draconis Volunteer Group! Engage in interstellar combat; steer your ship among the stars, and join your fellow fighter pilots in a quest for danger, glory, and victory.
