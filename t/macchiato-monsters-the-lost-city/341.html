Macchiato Monsters: The Lost City!

Gauntlet Calendar
RichardRuane March 5, 2019, 9:58pm #1

Separated from your caravan and lost without food or water in a desert wasteland, you find the statues of three gods standing over rocky steps descending into the heart of dune. Are they a mirage? Where will they lead?

Using the new edition of Eric Nieudan’s Macchiato Monsters, we’ll explore a classic pulp adventure from the 80s: The Lost City. The module emphasizes exploration, roleplay, and interaction as players work their way through a buried temple to the underground city below. There will be five sessions, and attendance at all sessions is strongly encouraged. Players should sign up for each session individually. The session will be recorded.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play, and we will use several Tools of the Table, including Lines & Veils and The X Card.
