On the new Gauntlet Podcast, hosts @swordqueen and @Auzumel_S talk with @RichRogers about Traverser, Fellowship 2e, and Rust Hulks. They discuss in-depth Rich’s “Star Wars Saturdays,” a project to run as many different games as possible in the SWU. Good stuff.

If you play on the Gauntlet you have a project or Kickstarter, give us a heads up a month or two beforehand. We’d love to use the podcast to support more Gauntlet Community members.
