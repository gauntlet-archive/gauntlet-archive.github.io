Time is out of joint, and its up to the Occult Heroes of Coven Prime to step out of the shadows once more to come the aid of New Gauntlet City while navigating the scheming factions of the supernatural world!

This is a 5-part series set in the Gauntlet Comics Coven Prime setting, our homage to the Vertigo occult heroes of the late 90s and early 2000s with a dash of Blade, Midnight Suns , and Clive Barker in the mix. Players may pick up playing already-established Coven Prime characters, or create a new PC to become part of the continuity. We’ll use Urban Shadows as our base system and tweak it as needed for comic book-style fun.

More information on Coven Prime can be found in the Gauntlet Comics WIKI.

We’ll use Safety Tools including Lines & Veils, Script Change, and Open Door to make sure we’re all having a scary good time. Please note that there will be 4 players in this series, one seat is being held for the person who requested the game.Please note that this event abides by the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

Sessions on Tuesday evenings in September at 8:00 PM Eastern US time.
