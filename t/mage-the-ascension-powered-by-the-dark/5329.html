This is for five sessions of Mage the Ascension: Powered by the Dark. MtA: PbtD is an older PbtA hack by Holden Shearer. You can find out more about there here: https://www.patreon.com/posts/powered-by-dark-19712458.

Knowledge of the Mage: The Ascension premise and world will be helpful. Attendance at all sessions is strongly encouraged. Mechanics for actual play will be taught. In session one, we’ll set up basics and safety, create characters, talk about what kind of game we want, and get to playing.

We will use Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
