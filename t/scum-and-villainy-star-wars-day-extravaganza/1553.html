STAR WARS DAY EXTRAVAGANZA

For many years fans of Star Wars have held May 4th as a day to celebrate the franchise. To celebrate the juggernaut of Nerd Culture we will be playing three sessions of Scum and Villainy set in the Star Wars Universe on this day.

We will be playing a total of 3 three hour sessions of Scum and Villainy on the 4th May using Roll20 and Google Hangouts. There will be minimal setup for this session and we will be using Pre-generated characters. These sessions will be recorded for Youtube.

The stories told in these sessions are linked but the ability to play in all sessions is not required. These sessions are meant for everybody to experience and celebrate Star Wars. I would ask that people sign up for a single session initially. If there are any spaces left as of the 17th if April, i will then open these for people to sign up for multiple sessions.

May the Force be with you.
