Once a selkie swam free in the ocean. Then someone stole their skin and held them captive for many long years. Now, the captor is dead and the skin has been found. The selkie and their surviving family must grapple with what to do next.

A one-session freeform LARP that explores the trauma and loss of a once-captive selkie who is finally free.

Session 1/1: April 13th, 7:30 pm EST https://gauntlet-hangouts.firebaseapp.com/event-detail/-M1q-P3dXlPg9Q9Ti3ZA

Game available here: https://iterateonwards.itch.io/sealskin

Content Warning : Sealskin focuses on the experiences of a selkie who was captured, forced into marriage, and made to raise children with their captor.
