Concept

A family from one part of the world moves to another part of the world, ostensibly to reinvent themselves, recover from a loss, or simply to start over. This is a game about self discovery, defining what it means to be home, and the strength of the family in trying times.

Aim

The characters explore the community and establish their place in it, navigating cultural differences, while being respectful and gracious.

Tone

The game strives to build sweet stories with sympathetic if flawed characters. The family may grate on each other but ultimately love and respect each other. The family may be foreign and clumsy, but ultimately they respect the community, its people, and its culture.

Subject Matter

The Family Abroad covers family relations that might sometimes strain under stress. It explicitly highlights cross-cultural relations and may wade into ignorance and prejudice. The purpose of the game is to maintain respect while navigating these differences.

At A Glance

  • Posting Recording: With consent from all players

  • Safety: Lines & Veils, X-Card, Open Table, and any others suggested by the players
