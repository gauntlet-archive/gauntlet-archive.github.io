Lost Caverns of Tsojcanth is pretty bonkers mega-ish dungeon and a favorite of mine from back in the day. It’s one of those modules that comes with an extra book of player-facing art!

If you can find it anywhere any more, Forgotten Temple of Tharizdun is also pretty weird, and ends with your party maybe letting a terrifying dead god back into the world for… reasons? I forget.
