Apocalypse World: Rusted Trust

Wednesdays in October at 7 pm CDT

Something is wrong with the world. Let’s find out what it is together.

This is a classic Apocalypse World game. Myself, Christopher W/Tuirgin, and three others are going to lego together a world that is broken, bent, and chipped in so many lovely ways. Will we have a flooded world with ship towns? Will the land have retaken the old cities, leaving us in forested abysses? Have the gods forsaken us or turned their ire to the world? What is the Psychic Maelstrom!?

This game is set for five sessions, three hours a piece, and we will make the world, then characters, and then fill it with all that delicious decay drama Apocalypse World promises.

For the first three days (until September 3rd) this is posted please only sign up if you have not had me as an MC before.
