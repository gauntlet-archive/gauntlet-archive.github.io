Pasión de las Pasiones - Esta Noche, Violenta y Sangrienta

Gauntlet Calendar
Puckett July 17, 2020, 8:48pm #1

For the first 48 hours, please only sign up if you are in the UK/EU

Note: The event day has been changed from Thursday at 2 p.m. Central time/GMT -5 to Friday at 2 p.m./GMT -5.

Crime lord Hugo Díaz Ventura has been taken into custody, but even as his gang disintegrated before his eyes due to the heroic actions of his twin, known (to most) only as The Fox, a third severed head, drained of blood, was found on another sidewalk in the usually sleepy coastal town called Ciudad Soñando. The mysteries remain - why are the heads drained of blood? And where are the bodies? Why haven’t they turned up yet?

Yet somehow, the nightlife continues, even with a serial killer dubbed El Sangrador - who has already claimed the lives of professional surfers Kevin Brewster and Jason Collins and rising star Esteban Ruiz - stalking the darkness. Even worse, Ventura’s former lieutenants are fighting to claim parts of his empire for themselves, and the echo of gunfire through the streets is becoming a sadly common sound. And winter storms approach, bringing bigger waves and more surfers … including a surf camp for young Black women, coming to town to spend a week surfing with legends like Martina Segovia, Monica Carlos, and Jade Tyler.

There’s an underlying tension in the neon-clad nightclubs now; the music isn’t quite as loud, people don’t order as many drinks, and they leave a little bit earlier, usually with at least one other person. There is, after all, safety in numbers. … Isn’t there?
