This is for the first of four sessions of Hearts of Wulin using the courtly mechanics add on. Characters will be playing political games within the swirling word of a court setting. Attendance at all sessions is strongly encouraged. Rules will be taught, and you don’t necessarily need experience in the genre, but a love for the intersection of machinations and romance will be helpful.

Media touchstones: Shadow, Curse of the Golden Flower, Judge Dee, Princess Agents, Legend of the Black Scorpion (aka The Banquet), The Rise of Phoenixes

We will be using Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
