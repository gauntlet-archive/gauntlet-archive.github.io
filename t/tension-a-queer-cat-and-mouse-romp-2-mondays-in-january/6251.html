Tension: A Queer Cat and Mouse Romp (2 Mondays in January)

Gauntlet Calendar

Donogh December 18, 2020, 1:48pm #1

Play as an investigator and a killer as they get entangled in each other, pursuing each other romantically and physically. Create a whole cast of characters in your orbit, building a map of relationships through play that begins to explode and fray in fabulous ways as people start dying!

Tension is not a mystery, it’s a dark comedy thriller romance. The killer and the investigator know who the other is, yet they are still inexplicably drawn together. Hijinks and drama ensue as they are trapped in a complicated web of attraction, lies, and even truth.

We’ll be using the Ensemble optional role (which I will be filling) to inhabit the orbiting characters whenever they are introduced.

Media Touchstones:
Killing Eve, Hannibal

Content Warning: Casual Death, Drug Use, Sex Work, Strong Language, and Imbalanced Power Dynamics
System: Tarot-Driven
Duration: Two 3 hour sessions
Breaks: Two 5-10 minute breaks
Venue: Facilitator’s Zoom
Recording: Yes, but made public only with all players’ enthusiastic consent!
Attendance: Please only RSVP if you can do so for both sessions.
Safety: Lines & Veils, Script Change, and Open Door Policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players. This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

RSVPs open to all from 21st December at 1pm (GMT)
