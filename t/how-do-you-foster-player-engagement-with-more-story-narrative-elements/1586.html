Similar to what @beepeegee was saying about about introducing plot elements relevant to character pasts, one of my favorite tools to bolt on to any game (which doesn‚Äôt already have some kind of collaborative backstory procedure built in, but partciularly trad-style games) is Backstory Cards. I consider it a Session Zero kind of thing, but upon reflecting, I guess there‚Äôs no reason it couldn‚Äôt be done mid-campaign!

The gist of it is a deck of cards with questions and a few randomizing elements have the party constructing a grid of setting elements (people, places, events) and the relationships between them.
