I don’t know SCP (to me it means ‘secure copy’) so I had to look it up. I guess you mean the Secure Contain Protect collaborative writing project. It uses a wiki to do its collaborative writing. Specifically they use the https://www.wikidot.com/ hosting service. They offer free accounts and offer very generous terms to “quality community wikis” – I don’t know what they mean by “quality” here.

Hope that helps to get you started.
