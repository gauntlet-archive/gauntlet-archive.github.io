Sign in Stranger: Making Contact

Gauntlet Calendar
Julian October 2, 2019, 10:22am #1

Your life on Earth is over.

First contact with alien life has occured, at catastrophic risk. Humanity turns down the chance to join the galactic community, but some individuals choose to leave Earth to explore the universe now open to them.

You play one of those who leave Earth, never to return. Changing and being changed by the new world they enter.

Enter a strange new world.

Sign in Stranger is a collaborative, long-term role-playing game by Emily Care Boss. Working together, players create a truly alien environment using mechanics inspired by mad-libs: random words are chosen as creative spring-boards for describing the beings and world around the humans. Over the course of weeks or months, the game takes the characters from exploring their nearby surroundings to taking part in politics and intrigue ranging across stellar systems.

We will be playing the game over four sessions. This is session 1 of 4. Being able to attend each session is ideal, but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

This session is scheduled for 3 hours. It will not be recorded.

The X-Card will be in play and we will abide by the Gauntlet’s Inclusivity Policy (http://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html).
