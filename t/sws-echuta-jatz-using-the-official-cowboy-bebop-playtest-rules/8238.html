Maybe you ran with the in-crowd. Maybe you ran into more trouble than creds. Maybe you just ran out of options.

Here you are, once a rising name as a Bounty Hunter or a wannabe Hunter.

Now’s your chance, or off to the Spice Mines of Kessel you’ll go, courtesy of your new and only employer, the Galactic Empire.

  • You are a group of Bounty Hunters who’ve gotten to know a little about each other while in a former Republic Judiciary Central Detention Center.
  • You’ve been recently paroled by the new Galactic Empire under the expressed condition that you will work for the Empire.
  • Your assignment is to hunt down traitors (members of the Rebel Alliance), such as Senator Jar Jar Binks and return them alive to Coruscant to face a fair and public trial.

This game is part of the ongoing Star Wars Saturdays series. The time setting is after the end of Episode III of the Star Wars movie series (that’s Revenge of the Sith ) when the Galactic Empire is rising and the Rebel Alliance is working to become a united front to stop the Empire. Characters and settings created in this miniseries may exist in the SWS canon for future miniseries and may be added to the wiki (https://starwarssaturdays.miraheze.org/wiki/Main_Page).

CW: Violence, Imperial power abuse, adult themes, madcap twists
System : Cowboy Bebop Roleplaying Game – Playtest Prototype
Duration : One 4 hour session, which includes character creation
Breaks : 3 breaks, about once an hour (10 mins)
Venue : StreamYard
Recording : Yes, but made public only if consent is given by all players

The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct will be in play and safety tools (Script Change and X-Card) will be in use.
