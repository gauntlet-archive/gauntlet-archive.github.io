Alien: Chariots of the Gods


NOTE: For this game, you should be able to sign up for all 3 sessions.

A haul of possible dangerous isotopes that are used for fuel is in the cargo hold of your ship. It pays well because usually these kinds of fuel are towed behind, but they needed it delivered and you needed the money.

When you come out of Cryo, you’re sure it’s going to be a regular job, just like dozens you’ve done before. Until you find out you’ve got a bit of company.

The Alien RPG is by Free League Publishing and published by Modiphius. Welcome to this tense, easy to learn game of thriller films that may or may not have a bit of something…non human…up its sleeve.

There are 3 sessions that are 3.5 hours each.

We’ll be using Zoom for our sessions and I’ll be recording, if everyone’s okay with that. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct (https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html) will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card (http://tinyurl.com/x-card-rpg) for safety tools.
