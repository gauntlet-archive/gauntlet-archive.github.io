Samurai, Ninja, Gamblers, and other anthropomorphic animals wander 16th Century Japan.

Powered by the Apocalypse and based on the works of Stan Sakai, Usagi Yojimbo’s most recent RPG uses support and setbacks as the characters get involved in the mechanization of Lords who don’t stop to think how it’ll touch the lives of those below them.

You do not need to be at all 4 sessions, but you should be available for at least 2 in a row.

NOTE: If you played in the May sessions, these will be different than the May games, so you’re welcome to join in.

There are 4 sessions that are 3.5 hours each.

We’ll be using Zoom for our sessions and I’ll be recording, if everyone’s okay with that. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct (https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html) will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card (http://tinyurl.com/x-card-rpg) for safety tools.
