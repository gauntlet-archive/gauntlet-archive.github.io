You are the crew of a spaceship, trying to keep flying and make ends meet while under the iron-fisted rule of the Galactic Hegemony. You are in the Procyon sector, on the far edge of known space. This far out, there is less of a Hegemonic presence, and local powers include pirate queens, criminal syndicates and legitimate corporate interests. Your crew takes what jobs it can, both legal and illegal.
