Role - new tabletop rpg platform

Hype
Kyle_Simons June 3, 2020, 4:33am #1

There’s a pretty snazzy looking roll20 challenger that launched on kickstarter today that has got me pretty psyched called ROLE: https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/role/role-a-new-kind-of-platform-for-the-rpg-community

If that drag-an-drop customizable character sheet works it’s already a game-changer for me as a small publisher because getting a nice automated character sheet on a platform like roll20 is a popular ask these days but it’s actually pretty expensive if you can’t do it yourself (like me).

Love the focus on diversity, safety tools, more robust player matching options, as well as the other standard stuff you’d need for running online.
