If you will not be able to attend all 4 sessions it would be best to wait and attend a Game Facilitator Camp when your schedule is more open.

Hi there! I’m Alexi Sargeant, and I facilitate games on the Gauntlet Calendar. And I want you to, too! In July I will be coaching four sessions of Game Facilitator Camp: one part support group, one part crash course on facilitating TTRPGs online.

During GFC I will be offering to help out new GMs run their first one-shot of a game of their choosing for the Gauntlet Calendar.

In this initial session I will walk you through the steps of starting a video call, creating events on Firebase, and sharing your events through the official channels. I will demonstrate how to use resources like rollwithme.xyz and the Play Aids folder. We will go over the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct and safety tools.

In the next three weeks, each participant will facilitate a one-shot session. I will attend each of these games myself, along with your fellow campers. Each one-shot will take place on the same day of the week and time as this event. First come, first served for reserving days for one-shots. When choosing what to run for your first session, pick a game that you feel comfortable with, that you find personally exciting, and that can support a number of players equal to the other participants and coach (that’d be two other participants and one coach). It can even be a GM-less game! I will work with you to make sure you have everything you need to facilitate the game online.

I look forward to learning and playing together this July!
