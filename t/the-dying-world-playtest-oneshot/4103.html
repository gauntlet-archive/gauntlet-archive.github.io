For the first 72 hours of access, please sign up for the game if you are a member of a marginalized community. Thank you!

This session is scheduled for 4 hours. The session will be recorded if everyone is cool with it! If not, no worries. We will be using Streamyard. I will email everyone one week ahead of the game.

The multiverse has shattered! And in this shattering, would be heroes rise. Imbued with powers of the Shattering, they have one last chance to save their worlds!

Play as one of five archetypes: The King of Cups, the Queen of Swords, the Princess of Wands, the Prince of Pentacles, and the Scion of the Void. The system is based on Grant Howitt’s Royal Blood, and uses the tarot to create characters, and determine task resolution. The stakes are high and feel high in this one shot!

Please note that this is a playtest! I’ll do my best to provide an awesome experience but there may be some hiccups.

Safety tools: We will be using script change, lines and veils, and an open door policy. We can change these tools to whatever suits the table best. This game follows The Gauntlet’s Inclusivity Policy.
