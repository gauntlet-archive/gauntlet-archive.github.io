[my own Dark Age hack of Monster of the Week]
Sundays in July at 4pm BST (GMT+1), These events are scheduled to run for 3 hours and will be recorded.

Being able to attend all sessions is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

The Anglo-Saxon settlement of Synhamm is in need of allies. Can the PCs reach a neighbouring Celtic kingdom and convince it to ally with Synhamm? Will they take Fate by the throat and make their own Destiny? Will they step into the Dark and bring the Light? Will they Forge their stories with Blood and Iron?

The playbooks available in this hack are: the Commoner; the Hammer of the Gods; the Loon; the Monster Hunter; the Outlaw; the Pagan Mystic; the Priest of God; the Scholar; and the Warrior of God.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and X-Card) will be in use.
