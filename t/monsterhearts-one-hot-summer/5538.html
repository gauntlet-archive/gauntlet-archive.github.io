This is five sessions of Monsterhearts 2e. This will be set in a summer resort in the forest and mountains of New England with the PCs playing late 18-21 year olds (or at least looking that old!) working as staff at an upmarket vacation resort of family cabins, a luxury spa and plenty of secrets. Each session will cover a week or two of game time; though the first session will be more of a session zero. The PCs themselves may be locals or been recruited from other countries to run specific activities.

This will be a player-first, highly collaborative game and the action will focus on the PCs’ agendas. External adversity and mysteries will support this focus rather than having being a driving force themselves. I will be asking players to portray NPCs in certain circumstances so as to keep everyone involved as much as they wish. The game will be a spin-off of the Halte setting created in a prior Gauntlet game. The game will use the latest version of the Eternal Love subsystem which encourages PCs to find their soulmate.

Technical details
Note that this game will be played over audio chat and supporting shared documents. The audio may be retained for reference, but I do not intend to release it publicly and will not do so without all players’ permission. I may release a summary of play at some point afterwards.

This event is scheduled to run for 3 hours. There will be 5 sessions in total. Being able to attend each session is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct will be in play and X-Card) will be in use.
