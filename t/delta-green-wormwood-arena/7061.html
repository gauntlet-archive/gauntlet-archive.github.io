Wormwood Arena is a scenario for Delta Green in which the agents infiltrate a seemingly harmless New Age cult to investigate the disappearance of three cult members.

Delta Green is a horror roleplaying game. There will be violence and gore. Wormwood Arena specifically deals with themes of psychological manipulation and mind control. We will be using the X-Card, Lines and Veils and CATS. The game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct. We will be using streamyard. The session will be recorded but only made public if all players agree. I will send a reminder per email a week before we play.

This is part of the Gauntlet Delta Green shared world. Surviving agents will be added to the roster at the end of the series.
