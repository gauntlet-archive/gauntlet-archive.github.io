PLEASE NOTE: This game is a playtest. Please only sign up if you are interested in play testing and comfortable being recorded.

Blood Ties is a Powered by the Apocalypse game with roots in Hearts of Wulin and Brindlewood Bay for stories about witches living, loving, and fighting evil in their quaint New England hometown. Play as the heirs to powerful magical dynasties, each with their own special brand of witchcraft. Inspired by stories like Practical Magic, Witches of East End, Charmed, and The Secret Circle , you create your own world of drama, suspense, romance, and enchantment.

Content Notes: This game may explore difficult themes: dysfunctional relationships (romantic and familial), romance and sexuality,

Duration : Four 3 hour sessions

Breaks : 2 breaks per session (5-10 mins)

Venue : GM’s Zoom

Recording : Yes, but made public only if consent by all the players

Attendance : Joining all four sessions is ideal but not required

Safety : Lines & Veils, Script Change, an open door policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players.

I will email everyone a week ahead to confirm attendance.
