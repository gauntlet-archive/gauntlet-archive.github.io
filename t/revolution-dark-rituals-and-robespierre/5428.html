Révolution: Dark Rituals and Robespierre

4 players total including myself. All access time for this session is 2020-06-17T23:00:00Z.

Révolution is a game of dark magic, political intrigue, and desperate romances set against the backdrop of the French Revolution. We will each play as powerful, influential, wealthy, or magically inclined players on the Parisian political landscape and will each be vying for power. The game is GMless and is a take on the Belonging Outside Belonging framework.

Tone and setting touchstones: Les Misérables, Game of Thrones, Assassin’s Creed: Unity, Faust

This first session will be primarily character and setting creation, with a brief set of role play opportunities to test drive the characters.

This is the first of four sessions. Attendance at all sessions is encouraged but not required. The game will be played over Zoom and will be recorded/shared online with player permission.

Safety Tools

We will be using script change, x-card, lines and veils, and an open door policy. All of these tools will be explained at the start of the session. This game follows The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
