Hey, welcome!
I did something simiilar for a middle school advisory/excursion activity. Drama teacher here so, of course, right?
I didn‚Äôt have as many students, about 15. I grouped them in 3 to 4 around a A4 printout of different characters from https://2minutetabletop.com. But I also suggested ‚Äúroles‚Äù for each of them, with lots of blank paper and pencils.
