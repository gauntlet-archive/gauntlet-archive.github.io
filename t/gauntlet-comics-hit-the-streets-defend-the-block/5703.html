Hit the Streets: Defend the Block is about street-level vigilantes. Think Marvel series on Netflix like Daredevil and Luke Cage.

299065739×1080 374 KB

The game takes place in New Gauntlet City, a modern metropolis with diverse neighborhoods, a destabilizing concentration of wealth, and significant street-level crime.

You will portray the Super Powered Beings of a particular neighborhood in the NGC. You’ll fight criminals, butt heads with rivals, and lend a hand to help your neighborhood thrive.

This game is part of the ongoing Gauntlet Comics continuity. Characters and settings created in this miniseries will exist in the Gauntlet Comics canon (and on the wiki).

Safety tools: We will use the X-Card and Lines & Veils.

Attendance at every session is not required, but please RSVP for each session you plan to attend.
