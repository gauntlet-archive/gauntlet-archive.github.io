Markfullyarmoured April 6, 2023, 3:58am #477

Heya! First time poster after long time listener to Jason Cordova on various channels.

I’m working on a game called KNIGHT. People are Arthurian style knights, questing about the land for the High Queen of Avalon. The game involves collab style gaming, where players are creating a map filled with adventure points between them before going back to their knightly house and doing various things. It’s also got a generational aspect - knights only get about ten adventures before they retire, to which you play your heir.

It’s rules light, with a mix between procedures as in Blades in the Dark and a player facing version of Into the Odd. I was influenced strongly by Trophy’s consequences, but have yet to integrate anything with it. Had a few playtests and ran a full campaign of 30 sessions with my after-school kids.

The process has taught me the pain and joy of layout design and youtube tutorials for InDesign :D.

If people are interested I have a webpage and a discord .

Hope to chat, here or elsewhere!
