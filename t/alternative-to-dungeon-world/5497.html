I can say that, with close to 100 sessions under belt (both playing and running) DW-adjacent games that use advantage instead of +1 forward, that there have been no shortages of misses, and definitely no shortages of 7-9 results pushing the stories.

Yes, advantage is a significant boost. When you‚Äôre starting with a +0 or +1 modifier, it‚Äôs closer to a +2 than a +1. It‚Äôs more like a +1 than a +2 when you start off at +2 or +3 in the first place. Reasons I like advantage:

  • Failure is always at least a little possible
  • It‚Äôs very clear whether a +1 bonus only actually affected a roll, and only ~1 in 4 rolls are actually affected. So more often than not, it feels like that +1 modifier you got ‚Äúdidn‚Äôt matter.‚Äù
  • It‚Äôs much less clear whether advantage affected any given roll (even if you roll a special die, it still feels like it might have mattered. Thus, advantage feels like more of a boost.
