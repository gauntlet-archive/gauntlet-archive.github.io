Gauntlet Comics—Masks: AEON After-School

I will be running three sessions of Masks in May on Fridays at 8pm Eastern.

AEON After-School Alliance for At-Risk Supers is a program AEON put together to keep young superheroes constructively occupied. You, young hero, are lucky enough to get to be part of the pilot program. You might be a rebel AEON wants to keep an eye on, or a goody-two-shoes charged with keeping the others on the straight-and-narrow. Whatever your role, your success as a team in cleaning up Undertaker’s Row in New Gauntlet City will determine if this AEON program vaults to success or goes down in flames.

This game is part of the ongoing Gauntlet Comics series—thank you @richrogers and @JimLikesGames for letting me play in the sandbox!
