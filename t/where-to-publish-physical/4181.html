I’ve had some success since publishing a PDF on DriveThruRPG, and now I’m ready to release my next title as a physical zine as well. Where do people like to do this? The options I’ve explored all require a monthly subscription of some sort; is this just a cost of doing business, or is there a popular portal through which I can accept payments and fulfill shipping myself?

Michael December 16, 2019, 5:38pm #2

Weebly has a free tier and processes transactions through Square. Handy if you want to use square for in-person transactions.

If you are ok with a simple ‘Buy Now’ button, Paypal Buttons are easy to embed in a page you already have. My site uses Paypal buttons currently.

Gumroad has a free tier as well and I like their clean layout. You can even embed items in other sites (Wordpress etc). I’m looking at switching to Gumroad, for the better tracking of items and deeper user features.
