Join us on the set of the production of the Movie Casa del Toro (House of the bull) as production moves along in the deserts outside Ciudad Sonando in sunny Baja California. The film is over budget, the actors are a secret mess of conflicting emotions and passions.

Whom will declare their love to whom, what secrets be revealed, what shocking scandals will come to light, Tune in next week to everybody’s favour telenovela, La Dama de Corazones, La Jota de Picas.

We will be playing 4 sessions of Pasión de las Pasiones. the sessions will run for 3 hours with a break planned for every hour…

We will be using the X-card, Lines and Veils as safety tools.

Content warning: Please be aware, Pasiones de las Pasiones is a game about romance and passions and Melodrama and it is important to consider this that when you think about playing.
