Very happy to present the completed weather system today. Here‚Äôs the link: https://gwyllgi.itch.io/forecast

The way this works is simple but really fun. Players have six double-sided cards, each representing a full day‚Äôs weather. Either side of the card can be used for the day or night. OK, here‚Äôs the short form:
You choose what you care for most: camping or scouting.
You roll 2D6: the best score is for the thing you care for.
On 1-2, mark a negative condition ; on a 5-6 mark a positive condition.
