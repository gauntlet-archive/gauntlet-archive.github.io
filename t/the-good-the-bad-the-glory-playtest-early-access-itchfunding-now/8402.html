You’re a gunfighter. For money or justice, it don’t make no difference—fame and Glory mean just as much, maybe more. If you can’t find none at this town, you’ll try again at the next. Heck, you’ll ride clear across the frontier if you have to…lucky you ain’t got to ride alone.

System: THE GOOD, THE BAD & THE GLORY (by V2S Games) is a wild west expansion for the PARAGON system from the AGON roleplaying gam e. For more on the PARAGON system visit AGON-RPG.com. ITCHFUNDING NOW. (No experience with the system is necessary, rules will be taught. Please see below for more information)

Content warnings : Possible themes include guns, violence, crime, fire, murder, injustice, loss and death.

Touchstones: AGON (ttrpg), Deadwood (TV series), Red Dead Redemption (video game series), The Man with No Name Trilogy (films), Dogs in the Vineyard (ttrpg), Blades in the Dark (ttrpg) & more.

Sessions: 4. Being able to attend each session is strongly requested; please RSVP to each session.

Duration : 4 hrs

Breaks : 5-10 min breaks on the hour.

Venue : Zoom (a link will be sent via email).

Recording Session : With consent from all players

Posting Recording : With consent from all players

Safety Tools: Lines & Veils, X-Card and Script Change. This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct and the Open Door Policy is in place, meaning anyone should feel free to leave the game, or cancel as needed.

Play aids: A Gunfighter Character Keeper & Play Aid (Google Sheets), will be provided via Google Drive. For playing cards, we’ll be using https://playingcards.io.

Communication: Confirmation emails will be sent 1 week prior to the event and again 48hrs in case of any last minute changes.
