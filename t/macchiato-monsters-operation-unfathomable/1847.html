We will be playing three sessions of Operation Unfathomable by The Hydra Cooperative, using Macchiato Monsters. Due to high lethality, we will be creating two PCs in the first session.

The tone of the module is a gonzo pulp comedy mashup of science fantasy and high fantasy. All subject matter is treated ironically and unseriously.

We will be using Script Change by Brie Sheldon (http://www.briecs.com/p/script-change-rpg-tool.html) as well as Lines and Veils for safety.
Please review the content warnings to see if you feel comfortable, as it will be impossible to make this adventure anything other than gonzo. I will do my best to accommodate all players in the group.

We will be using Roll20 to manage the map and images, and Google Sheets for our character keeper.
