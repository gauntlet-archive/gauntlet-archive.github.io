Young shift workers in the graveyard hours of Shop Smart!

Shop Smart is a big-box store just outside of town. A strange town, with strange customers - but that’s okay, Shop Smart has some strange employees!

A limited selection of Skins is available for the players (as per the usual Small Town setups) Harpy, Hollow, Infernal, Mortal, Queen, Wyrm; though other Skins are possible if the players want to branch out.

Media Touchstones:
Clerks, Go, Super Store

Content Warning: Teenage Monsters, Bad Managers & Worse Customers, Shiftwork
System: PbtA
Duration: Four 3-hour sessions
Breaks: One 10-15 minute break in the middle (but more if required)
Venue: Facilitator’s Zoom
Recording: Yes, but made public only with all players’ enthusiastic consent!
Attendance: Being able to attend each session is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.
Safety: Lines & Veils, Script Change, and Open Door Policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players. This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

RSVPs open to all from 13th November at 1pm (GMT)
