(Playtesting Weekend) Vigilance Fantasy RPG

Gauntlet Calendar
DavidMK December 27, 2021, 12:27am #1

Part man. Part myth. All Law.

Inspired by mythology and Robocop, Vigilance is an RPG about Living Laws in a Post-Bronze Age Collapsed Fantasy World.

Pregenerated characters and their province will be supplied.

Note: Playtesting of resolution mechanics—looking for testers who understand dice pools and probability.

This event is scheduled to run for 2 hours. It will be recorded assuming all parties agree.

The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct will be in play and safety tools (Script Change and X-Card) will be in use.
