Girl Underground (4 games on Sundays in November)

“Oh, good morning, young lady! You look a wee bit discombobulated … yes, I am a dragon. Sort of. A kettledragon, on account of my mother having been a teakettle. What do you mean, that’s ridiculous? You have leaves in your hair and earth on your dress, and neither seems quite intentional, so who’s ridiculous, really? No, I don’t know how you got here. Neither do I know the way to your home - maybe you should as an oracle? You don’t know any oracles? You are extraordinarily uninformed, I think, so I will help you find the oracles of the west, south and east. What? North? Oh no, you don’t want to go North, young lady. Certainly not!!”

We will play four sessions of Girl Underground, a game by Jesse Ross & Lauren McManamon about a girl who falls into a fantastic world, meets strange companions, and learns about the world and herself. The girl is a shared character, but each player will also play a companion of their very own!

Jammi will be a guest star for these games.

Being able to attend all sessions is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

No experience is needed; everything will be taught in the game.

Venue : Zoom

Contact : Players will receive an e-mail to check in about a week before the start of the game.

We will discuss Content, Aim, Tone, and Safety Tools in the first sessions. We will use Lines & Veils, the X-Card, and the Open Door policy, and I will prepare a character keeper as a shared document.
