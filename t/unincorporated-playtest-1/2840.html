An optimistic person might call this place a town. It has a name. It just doesn’t have many people. Which makes these other losers who are still hanging around important. They’re the folks you see every day, the people you work with, the people you drink with at the bar. “Friends” might be a stretch, but like it or not, they’ve known you forever. They know all the stupid things you’ve done. Maybe it’s been weeks, maybe decades, but they won’t let you forget. Nobody gets a blank slate here.

Unincorporated is a GMless story game about ordinary people caught up in petty dramas, long-standing grudges, and hopeless love affairs, under the weight of reputations and baggage they’re trying to shake off.

This is a playtest of a very rough draft, so expect some hitches and on-the-fly rule adjustments!
