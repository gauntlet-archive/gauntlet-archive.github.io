We are more than excited to playtest Ma Nishtana: Why is this Night Different? again on the Gauntlet!

Ma Nishtana is a ritual experience inspired by the Passover Seder and the story of Hebrew Exodus from Egypt.You need not be religious nor Jewish to play. This is a rotating-gm style story game in which we play to examine the magic of ritual and collective action.

This is a game in development and we are looking for players to give feedback to make this a more welcome game for everyone. The play session itself, including character creation and rules workshop is expected to last about 2.5 hrs with about 15 minutes left over for discussion and reflection.

The story we tell together may deal with themes of loss, subjugation, violence, and deprivation. To navigate this tonal landscape we will be using lines and veils**, a version of scriptchange, and an open-door policy will be in effect. We will not be recording. The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct will be in play.

In the email we send on August 12, we will include a link to the character keeper so you can review and adjust the lines and veils.

We will be meeting over Zoom, the link of which will be sent in the email.
