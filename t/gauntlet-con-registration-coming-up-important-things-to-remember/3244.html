RichardRuane September 22, 2019, 7:14pm #1

Hey all - Gauntlet Con 2019 registration is coming up. Here’s some important things to remember.

If you don’t complete both steps, you’ll risk not being confirmed as an attendee for registration on Sept. 28.

  1. You must be a Gauntlet Patreon Supporter by end of day tomorrow (2019-09-23T04:00:00Z)!
  2. You must log into the Gauntlet Con registration site by noon Eastern (NYC/Toronto) on 2019-09-28T16:00:00Z. Unless you are a guest or GM, please do this even if you had an existing account from last year.
  3. Once you’ve completed these steps, you should be able to complete registration on 2019-09-28T21:00:00Z.

Please know there’s nothing manual about this process. Your accounts on the registration site have to be created by you, and we can’t confirm you as an attendee until after you log in. We have to do the confirmations manually for each individual, so that’s why we need your help in completing both steps before the deadlines above.

Thank you!

Richard
