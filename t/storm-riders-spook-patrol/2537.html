Storm Riders! - Spook Patrol

Gauntlet Calendar
Luiz July 6, 2019, 9:29pm #1

These are four sessions, 3-hours each. Attendance of all sessions is fun, but not mandatory. You have to RSVP to each session you want to join.

SPOOK PATROL is the hit new cartoon sweeping the nation! The awesome land of Hallowoodia is under attack from the terrible Legion of Dawn, an army of uppity angels who wants to end the eternal night and fill the land with the burning rays of the sun! Only the SPOOK PATROL, the most frightening and friendly heroes of the land, can protect the innocent Hallowoodians and make sure their party never ends!

Storm Riders! is a game about 80s cartoons and the kids who loved them, featured in Codex - Joy 2. We’ll create spooky (but not really) heroes and the audience members who tune in every week to see them fight against evil! We’ll also occasionally play the network executives and the producers of the show, as well as come up with a bunch of fictional merchandise.

The X-card will be used, and we will be observing the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy (http://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html).
