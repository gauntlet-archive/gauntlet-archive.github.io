I agree! Concerning the lessons about teamwork I have with my seven to eight year old students I made a little game where they have to prepare a surprise birthday party for mom before she returns from work. No dice rolling but using actions as a resource. Doing something together often makes it twice as quick, but you can also do two different things at the same time instead. And some things need two or more people to do. (like moving a couch or something). They have to finish the preparations before the 'mom-returns" timer/clock is filled.

Maybe one of these days I get into writing it up and lay-outing it, and perhaps even translating it to English.
