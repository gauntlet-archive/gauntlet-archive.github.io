Camp Flying Moose for Girls of All Kinds is a PbtA game about teen girls at a summer camp filled with strange supernatural occurrences. Inspired by Lumberjanes, and the author’s own experiences of summer camp, the game investigates mysteries, monsters, and teenage identity.

Each session has a mystery to solve related to a strange event occurring at camp. Use your skills, friendship, and empathy to help supernatural creatures solve their problems!
