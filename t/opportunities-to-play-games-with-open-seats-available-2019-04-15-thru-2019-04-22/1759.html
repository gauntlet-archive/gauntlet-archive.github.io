Hey folks!

In this post, we will highlight games for the upcoming week which have open seats and therefore the opportunity to add some more gaming fun to your week.

Exodus: What Comes After Hallelujah

Monday, April 15, 2019 6:00 PM → Monday, April 15, 2019 9:00 PM
Exodus is a game by Erika Shepherd about a road trip from hell to someplace else, of angels running from a fascist dystopia, making community as they go, trying to find something better. It uses Avery Alder’s Belonging Outside Belonging system, as seen in Dream Askew/Dream Apart and is heavily inspired by Ezra Furman’s Transangelic Exodus. It’s a game rooted in a time, an autobiography and a plan and a dream and a fear all in one. Too many people, today, can imagine having to pick up one day and leave your life behind, to go on the run from a world that has no place for you. Exodus tells that story.

Slings & Arrows

Tuesday, April 16, 2019 10:00 PM → Wednesday, April 17, 2019 1:00 AM
@Mikel_Matthews’ Slings & Arrows is about theatre people and how the show they’re in seeps into their day jobs and personal lives.

Lacuna Part 1: The Creation of the Mystery and The Girl from Blue City

Tuesday, April 16, 2019 11:00 PM → Wednesday, April 17, 2019 2:00 AM
Lacuna Part 1. The Creation of the Mystery and the Girl from Blue City is an experimental roleplaying game . . . and you’re part of the experiment whether you realize it or not.

The Crimson Shadow of a Golden Cross playtest

Tuesday, April 16, 2019 11:00 PM → Wednesday, April 17, 2019 2:00 AM
This game is set in an original setting based on the Gothic Horror genre. It is meant to tell stories of religious corruption, class struggle, cultural oppression and grey morality.

Mecha Monthly: Armour Astir

Thursday, April 18, 2019 6:00 PM → Thursday, April 18, 2019 9:00 PM
A high-fantasy take on mecha warfare, play as rebellious magicians, fighters, clerics, and forty-foot tall magical constructs called Astirs, caught up in the struggle against an Authority that seeks to control them.

Things From The Flood

Friday, April 19, 2019 12:00 AM → Friday, April 19, 2019 3:00 AM
Things from the Flood is the sequel to Tales from the Loop. You play Teens in the 1990s – a decade of change and disaster. You’re still balancing day to day life with solving exciting mysteries with your friends. But this is a darker time, and the stakes are higher…

Storm Riders: GRUNTS

Friday, April 19, 2019 12:00 AM
When Freedom is threatened, when Liberty is under siege, when the forces of evil are knocking on your door, that’s when the GRUNTS (General Reserve United Nations Tactical Squad) mobilize! A Gauntlet Comics presentation! We’ll use @Michael_G_Barford’s Storm Riders to recreate the adventures of Solomon Toys’ massively popular 80s military action figures and the associated mega-hit afternoon cartoon (Look to GI Joe, MASK, and Green Plastic Army Men for inspiration).

Ironsworn (Co-op): Tales of the Iron Gauntlet Brotherhood

Saturday, April 20, 2019 8:00 AM → Saturday, April 20, 2019 4:00 PM
You are a hero doing quests between the Iron lands, the territories unclaimed by no clans or civilization. You are a member of the Iron Gauntlet Brotherhood, a loose-confederation of survivors, taking in warriors that outlasted their friends, peoples, bands or units.
