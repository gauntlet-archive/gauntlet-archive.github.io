PLEASE WAIT UNTIL ALL ACCESS TIME TO REGISTER IF YOU PLAYED YOUR FIRST GAUNTLET GAME BEFORE SEPTEMBER 1, 2020. I’m creating priority space for new members in this run.

Prom is tomorrow, today’s a math test and an alien invasion, and your powers have only just returned after the Battle of Band Trip. Good thing your lab partner is an extraterrestrial princess and your best friend is a secret vigilante.

Super Destiny High School Rumble!! is an anime-inspired tabletop RPG that uses the Apocalypse World Engine for fast character creation and rules-light, collaborative storytelling.

Note: This game series will allow players aged 13 and up.

This game is part of the ongoing Gauntlet Comics series. Characters and settings created in this miniseries will exist in the Gauntlet Comics canon (and on our wiki: https://gauntletcomics.miraheze.org/wiki/Main_Page) for future miniseries.

CW: Violence. Strong emotional ties.
System : Powered by the Apocalypse
Duration : Two 3 hour sessions
Breaks : 1 mid-point break per session (10 mins)
Venue : GM’s Zoom
Recording : Yes, but made public only if consent by all the players
Attendance : Joining all sessions is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.
Safety : Lines & Veils, X-card, and open door policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players. This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
