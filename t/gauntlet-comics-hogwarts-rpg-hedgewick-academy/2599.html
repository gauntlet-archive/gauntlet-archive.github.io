Young mages in New Gauntlet City learn to channel their magic at a boarding school founded by the wizard Vigilant of Coven Prime, Oliver Hedgewick. Can you and your friends solve the magical mysteries at your school?

We’ll be playing the Hogwarts RPG, reskinned for Gauntlet Comics, on two Wednesdays in August.

Being able to attend each session is ideal, but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend. A slot in each session is reserved for Sarah J.

Hedgewick Academy is an All-Ages series, albeit a spooky one—Gauntlet Comics’ answer to DC’s Gotham Academy. Expect peril and scares consistent with the early Harry Potter books. The X-Card, Open Door Policy, and Lines and Veils will be in use. This event abides by the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy.

Gauntlet Comics—Hogwarts RPG: Hedgewick Academy (1/2)
Young mages in New Gauntlet City learn to channel their magic at a boarding school full of mystery and mischief! Arsenius “Arsenic” Adebayo of House Raptorquill finds himself seeing sinister birds everywhere after a spell goes wrong. Noah Ford of House Burrowdown spots tell-tale signs of a time loop and gets dragged to the headmaster’s office to testify. Adrementitia Grey of House Lionheart hexes a fellow student to sing his heart out, then charges through the fire after a friend. Coriander Tauris of House Serpentine hides and sneaks away from professors and fiery sphinxes.

Gauntlet Comics—Hogwarts RPG: Hedgewick Academy (2/2)
Students at New Gauntlet City’s premier school of wizardcraft and witchery get in way too deep. Adrementitia is thrown into the trials of Coven Prime early. Noah is entranced by the possibilities of the Shroud of Mortality. Arsenius (“Arsenic”) solves the mystery of the birds with a little help from his ensorcelled cat. And Coriander discovers some older students’ secrets and resorts to blackmail.
