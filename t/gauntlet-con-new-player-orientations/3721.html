Hey All:

If you’re new to playing with the Gauntlet or to online games in general, we’ve got orientation sessions for Gauntlet Con on Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday. Orientation will include a chance to get to briefly meet other attendees, learn to troubleshoot the most common technologies we’ll be using, and learn about our standards around inclusion, preventing harassment, and using safety tools.

We’ve got new player orientations for Gauntlet Con set up on the RSVP site. Please reshare widely:

  • Wednesday Night NYC Time: Team Thracia! (2019-10-24T01:00:00Z)
  • Thursday Afternoon NYC Time: Team Unicorn! (2019-10-24T17:00:00Z)
  • Friday Afternoon NYC Time: Team Werebear! (2019-10-25T17:00:00Z)
