The Freedom Five!

After the events of OblivAeon, the Freedom Five through they’d earned a good rest.

NOT SO!

Legacy, Tachyon, Wraith, Bunker, Absolute Zero, and their intern, Unity, were relaxing in Megalopolis when a cry for help was heard! The Multiverse has been saved but the City needs more protecting!

This is the 6 issue series that comes with the Sentinel Comics RPG starter kit. You’ll be torn through time as you deal with the most recent threat to the Multiverse.

There will be 4 sessions total.

We’ll be playing on zoom and roll20. We will be recording for youtube actual plays, after checking with everyone to make sure this is okay.

Safety tools will include x card and lines and veils. The game itself will feel like 1960-70s comic books, so the feel should be along those lines.

This game is run as part of the Gauntlet gaming community and adheres to the Community Code of Conduct https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/community-code-of-conduct.html
