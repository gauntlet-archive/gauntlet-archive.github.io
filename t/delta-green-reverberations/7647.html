Delta Green is a horror roleplaying game. There will be violence and gore. Reverberations specifically has themes of drug abuse and anti-Asian racism.

We will be using the X-Card, Lines and Veils and CATS. The game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct. We will be using jitsi for the video call. I will send a reminder per email a week before we play.

This is part of the Gauntlet Delta Green shared world. New and returning agents are equally welcome. One seat is reserved to a B B. Surviving agents new to the program will be added to the roster at the end of the series.
