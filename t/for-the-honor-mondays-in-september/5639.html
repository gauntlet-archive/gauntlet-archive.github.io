For the Honor is a Firebrands game of She-Ra and the Princesses of Power with the serial numbers lightly filed off. In this GMless story game, we will take on the roles of the princesses, knights, and adventurers of the planet Athera and explore the magical and ever-changing world, the violent factions that would tear it apart, and the love we have for each other.

This is a three session run for four total players. Feel free to drop in/out of this game as you need. The game will be played over Zoom and will be recorded/shared online with player permission.

Safety Tools

We will be using script change, x-card, lines and veils, and an open door policy. All of these tools will be explained at the start of the session. This game follows The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
