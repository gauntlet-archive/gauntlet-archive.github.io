Similar questions have been asked in the fiction world for about a decade or more now. With technology at the point where anyone can write a short story or book and publish it with minimal effort, it‚Äôs possible to make a little money even as a hobbyist. I don‚Äôt know about your background, but it‚Äôs not that hard to publish something as a pdf or print on demand.

You need to:

  1. Create the game
  2. Proofread
  3. Get art, if you want any
  4. Put it into layout

If you can do 3 and 4 yourself, it‚Äôs not that hard to publish. Honestly, it‚Äôs not. It can drain energy though, but I used Lulu and it wasn‚Äôt a hassle at all.
