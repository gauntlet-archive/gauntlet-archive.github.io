Against the Vampire Conspiracy - Bucharest (1/3)

Gauntlet Calendar
Alun_R November 11, 2021, 9:02am #1

‘Cam’ Duchamp, the former DGSE Hacker called for help when her partner went missing at the end of a previous Op. Answering the call is Jimmy Rook, deniable criminal asset for MI6, Sophia Manzanares, ex-Secret Service Driver, and Yelena Nikitichna Pushkina, who used to kill people for the SVR (and perhaps the Conspiracy).
After re-inserting the young woman Cam had rescued during that Op into the Conspiracy the team track her boss (and likely abductor) to an elegant villa on the outskirts of Bucharest. There’s a creepy scientist, an armoured limo, and dangerous driving. Then, Yelena notices something odd about the limo driver …

Alun_R November 18, 2021, 4:20pm #2

Against the Vampire Conspiracy - Bucharest (2/3)
We pick up with Yelena the former SVR Assassin confronting a supernatural Servitor in the front of an armoured limo, while Jimmy the former MI6 ‘deniable’ fails to intervene as expected. Meanwhile, Sophia, the team’s Driver, is incapacitated which means that Cam, the French Hacker, has to get her hands dirty - which is when former Edinburgh Detective, Inspector Susan McLoed, arrives to help out. There’s gunshots, an explosive car chase, and an interrogation, before the next Operation begins to take shape … the opportunity to find out more about the ‘Van Helsing lineage’, by infiltrating a Club that’s the front for the Conspiracy in Bucharest …
