Lasers & Feelings

A quick-play RPG based on the song by The Doubleclicks.

YOU ARE THE CREW OF THE INTERSTELLAR SCOUT SHIP RAPTOR. Your mission is to explore uncharted regions of space, deal with aliens both friendly and deadly, and defend the Consortium worlds against space dangers.

CAPTAIN DARCY has been overcome by the strange psychic entity known as Something Else, leaving you to fend for yourselves while he recovers in a medical pod.

What happens next? Play to find out!

Sessions: This is a one-shot of the one-page game by John Harper.

Players: Four, plus the facilitator.

Content Warnings: Romantic love, sci-fi violence, ‘adult situations’.

Media Touchstones: Star Trek’s various incarnations, Galaxy Quest, The Orville.

Safety Tools: X-Card, Lines and Veils, Open Door.

Code of Conduct: This event adheres to the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

Technology: Zoom, rollwithme.xyz, .

Breaks: A 5-10 minute break at the top of each hour.

Prior Experience Required: None - rules will be taught in play, and are available here – https://johnharper.itch.io/lasers-feelings

Recording: This session will be recorded for the facilitator’s use. If everyone would like to see it posted, we may post to Youtube.

I will send links and further information in an email one week before the game. Please check your SPAM folder if you don’t see anything from me by then.
