Best Times for Session?

RPG Chat
ancusohm February 6, 2020, 4:44pm #1

So, I post games on the Gauntlet calendar sometimes, and I’ve been wondering what times generally work best for people.

I know that’s a complicated question because of players’ different timezones. But, I’m curious, is there a consensus on which times work best for most people?

I hope it’s okay, but I made a little poll that asks what times work best for you all. The polls divides each day into six 4-hour blocks (in GMT). I had to set up a separate poll for each date. Please keep in mind the time change.

If, for example, you want to say you like to play from 7:00 PM to 11:00 PM EST on Mondays, you’ll want to select the 12:00 - 4:00 AM GMT on Tuesdays

Please let me know if you’d prefer a different format.
