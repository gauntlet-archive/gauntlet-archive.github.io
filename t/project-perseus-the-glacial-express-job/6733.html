Project Perseus: The Glacial Express Job

A one-shot Spies vs The Supernatural adventure! All aboard Switzerland’s Glacier Express for nazi-punching adventure! Secure the Stork and put an end to Project Rhapsody!

[Thanks to Jammi for letting me steal their posting for the calendar!]

This game is part of an open playtest to help improve the game. Please only sign up if you’re okay with providing feedback through a survey after the sessions!

Project: Perseus is a game about superspies and their handlers fighting in the shadows against supernatural conspiracies and rival agencies during the late '80s and early '90s. We play to find out if the Agents can outwit, outrun, or outfight their opponents while finding a balance of trust with their Operators—which may not be easy, as some Agents may not have the same objectives and agendas on the mission. This is a game inspired by superspy movies like James Bond or Atomic Blonde, classic government agency shows like Alias, Ghost in the Shell, or Witch Hunter Robin, and urban fantasy novels.

Please note: This is a game with asymmetric player roles. One of the players will be an Operator, while the rest will play as Agents.

CW: supernatural horror, romance, possible betrayal, violence.

System : Project Perseus (open playtest), rules will be taught in play.

Duration : A single three-hour session.

Breaks : 2 5-10 minute breaks at the top of each hour.

Venue/Tech : GM’s Zoom and Jon Grim’s Roll with Me. https://rollwithme.xyz

Recording : Yes, but only if all players consent to recording and publishing. (GM recording for reference).

Attendance : It’s a one-shot!

Safety : Lines & Veils, The X-Card, Open Door Policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players.

Open access : Public signs up are available 3/25/2021 15:22 US CDT.

This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
