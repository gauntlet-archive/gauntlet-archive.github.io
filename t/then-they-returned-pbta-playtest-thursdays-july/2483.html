This is for two sessions of a PbtA teen horror mystery hack. It aims to capture the feel of Things from the Flood, Castle Rock, Bubblegumshoe, It, and beyond. You play teens in New Mexico in 2002. You and your friends deal with problems and relationships, even as the darkness begins to reach into your lives.

This is a light-weight PbtA hack drawing basics from Monsterhearts, Hearts of Wulin, and The Veil. This is a playtest, but we’ll mostly be looking at the roughest level: i.e. does it function. In session one we will make up characters and start a scenario we will finish in session two.

Content warning: this is a horror game which includes teen issues. Sessions run 2 1/2 to 3 hours. We will record the session and use the X-Card for safety. If you sign up and your attendance or RSVP changes, please also send me a message at edige23 at gmail. I will send out a group email a few days before the session with set up and invite details.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and the X-Card) safety tool will be in use.
