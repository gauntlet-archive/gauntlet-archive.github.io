Coming soon: Back Again from the Broken Land—ZineQuest

A fantasy RPG about small adventurers sharing stories on the long walk home. Inspired by Tolkien, Powered by the Apocalypse. Zine Quest

Back Again from the Broken Land is a fantasy PbtA game about small adventurers sharing stories during the long journey home after a calamitous against the evil Doomslord. It is inspired by the more contemplative stretches of Tolkien’s The Lord of the Rings. The game was originally published in Codex: Home and playtested on The Gauntlet. We will be revisinng and expanding the game text into a full zine version for ZineQuest, with a Kickstarter running for the first two weeks of February. Sign up at the pre-launch page to know exactly when the Kickstarter begins!
