The Pitch

City of Whispers is a city fantasy game, powered by the Apocalypse, currently in development.

Players assume the role of city denizens in a world filled with dangerous magic, political intrigue, and personal entanglements.

In addition to generating characters, the first session entails creating the city and its factions.

Caveat

This game is currently in development. This is a play test. We will likely encounter issues with the rules, but I will make sure everyone has fun.
