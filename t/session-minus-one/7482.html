For me, Social contract is Session minus one. The axioms (for building the world) must be transcendental (=meta). I used to direct to Bankuei page, which is an early model of the genre, but there are more up to date models and they are much more widespread.

On the specific point of world building, I think the concept of fantasy = handwave can be really helpful. What I call ‚Äúfantasy‚Äù is the idea that the seeds we plant for world building will be considered real despite of incoherence. Fantasy is where the imaginary aspect supercedes realism (the ontological one, which says that the fictive world works somehow like ours, that it continues to exist, that laws of logic are valid in it, etc.) which can be better understood as realism = coherence.

To put it in plain words : when I say elves are immortals ‚Äúin fantasy‚Äù, I want to handwave the particular of elves genetics and family bonds. Trying to follow coherence on this matter will only lead to finding all sorts of wrinkles and stitches behind the scene and that‚Äôs off limits. That‚Äôs a specific flavour of immortal elves.

You can also play them ‚Äúin realism‚Äù. You can plant imaginary seeds of immortal elves and follow the coherence and growth of your elves ‚Äúin realism‚Äù : you get speculative fiction, that‚Äôs what Shock:SF does.
