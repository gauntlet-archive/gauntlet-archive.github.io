Play out angst filled stories of ghosts and lovers, regrets and the death gods that fetch you.

A question of why the gods of death like pondering our mortality, and the weird traverse souls make from here to - another side.

Slice of Life Anime + Shinigami AU

Each session will center around an NPC we’ll deliver and fleshing out our character relationships alongside it.

I will email everyone a week ahead to confirm attendance.

Here Lie Death Gods is an odd PBTA + BOB hybrid of a game and has a supernatural seen as mundane kind of tone. Light magical realism and situates death and the afterlife as like a cute mundane office, but also like trying to relieve karma n such.

Content Warning : This is a game that focuses on emotional relationships, regrets and necessary death.

System : Here Lie Death Gods (Powered by the Apocalypse)

Duration : Three 3 hour sessions

Breaks : 2 breaks per session (5-10 mins)

Venue : GM’s Zoom

Recording : None

Attendance : Joining all five sessions is ideal but not required

Safety : Lines & Veils, Script Change, an open door policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players.

This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
