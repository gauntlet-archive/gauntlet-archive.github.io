To celebrate 3 years of Rich Rogers’ wonderful dedication to Star Wars Saturdays, the Gauntlet is hosting 2 days of Star Wars themed gaming!

Events will take place across the weekend of 5th & 6th December 2020. Games will be played in a variety of systems, hosted by GMs from all across the globe.

This thread will act as a masterlist for the events and will be updated periodically as GMs post their games to the Gauntlet Calendar. This is to avoid spamming the Forum with multiple posts and to act as a single point of reference for all games.

I will edit this post with updates throughout the day. All times will be posted in GMT for consistency and personal ease, but the site should show you in the local time.
