@Judd and I are back with a new episode of The Sixth Ring! This is a fantastic episode, with lots of great discussion and tips. The first segment is about how Trophy Gold has changed the play culture of Judd’s D&D group. The second segment is about Cat Evans’s Last Night on Earth, a truly excellent Rooted in Trophy game that has strong World of Darkness vibes. The third segment continues our discussion of converting OSR modules, in particular strategies for dividing up the physical space of the dungeon in preparation to make sets out of it. The fourth segment is some dragon doodling. The last segment, as always, is our short-form actual play featuring the mannikin Wöd Dum. Check it out:
