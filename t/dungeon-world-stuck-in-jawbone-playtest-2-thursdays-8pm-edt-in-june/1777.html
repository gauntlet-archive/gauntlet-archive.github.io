Please sign up for either the May OR June series, not both.

Where did the missing wizard go? What does the weeping ghost want? Who are the bandits hiding in the sewer? What’s going on at the factory? What is wrong with this place?

You probably didn’t plan to stay long in a dying mountain town full of smog and secrets. But here you are, so you might as well make the best of it and figure out what’s going on. Maybe somebody will even pay you for it.

Note: The scenario contains implied/offscreen violence to children and domestic animals by default. We’ll discuss lines & veils in the first session and make modifications where needed.
