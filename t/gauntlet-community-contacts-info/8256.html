Gauntlet Community

The Gauntlet Gaming Community is an online gaming club featuring Indie, Story, and OSR ttrpgs. Part of our Patreon goes to supporting that. https://www.patreon.com/gauntlet

For more information on the community, see https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/online-gaming.html
You can see our calendar of games here: https://gauntlet-hangouts.firebaseapp.com/events

For questions about the community side, you can email gauntletcommunity@gmail.com

You can also follow or message the community on Twitter @gauntletrpg

You can find out about our free online gaming convention, Gauntlet Community Open Gaming here: https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/gauntlet-community-open-gaming.html

For resources for online play see: https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/online-gaming-resources.html

You can find out more about our community podcast here: https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/gauntlet-podcast-index.html

We also have a YouTube channel: https://www.youtube.com/c/TheGauntletRPG

We have a program supporting marginalized members of the gaming community and those suffering financial hardship, Gauntlet Gameway. Find our more about that here: https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/gauntlet-gameway.html

You can find our Community Code of Conduct here: https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/community-code-of-conduct.html
