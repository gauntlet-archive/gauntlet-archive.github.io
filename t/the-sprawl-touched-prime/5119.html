This is for four sessions of The Sprawl Touched Prime, a game of cyberpunk + fantasy. In session one we will set tone and safety, make characters, establish facts about our world, and get playing. Rules will be taught. Attendance at all sessions is encouraged, but not required.

The Sprawl is a mission-based game, but we will work to give room for role play segments.

In the TSTP the world has been subject to strange rift energies which have transformed human beings into strange new ancestries, while others have managed to harness the powers of the rifts to…

OK, who are we kidding. This is Shadowrun using The Sprawl. The book takes a more generic, serial numbers filed off approach, but we will go ahead and lean into things.

We will use Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
