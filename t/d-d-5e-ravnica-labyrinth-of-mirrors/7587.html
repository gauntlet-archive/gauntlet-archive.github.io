D&D 5e: Ravnica: Labyrinth of Mirrors

Gauntlet Calendar
edige23 November 9, 2021, 10:33pm #1

For the first 24 hours only sign up if you’re not currently signed up for one of my sessions.

This is for nine sessions of Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition set in the MTG world of Ravnica running Mondays in January and February. Characters will begin at 2nd level—classes from the Players Handbook with add-ons from The Guildmasters’ Guide to Ravnica will be in play. Attendance at all sessions is encouraged, but not really required. One seat is being held. General RSVPs open 11/11.

I will provide notes on the Ravnica add-ons in a player document. While the rules will be taught, recognize these are a little crunchier than the standard Gauntlet fare. OOH I tend to streamline things in play, so if you’re a stickler for rules accuracy—this may not be the game for you. Some familiarity with the Ravnica setting would be helpful—even just skimming a wikipage about the various guilds.

You’re welcome to put together your character ahead of time (once we’ve confirmed class picks). I will also have a set of pre-gens ready, one for each class not already chosen by a player. Note that we may modify racial picks and feats.

We will be using Roll20 for maps and character keeper—it’s just easier that way. You can use that roller or another. But we will be using Zoom for the video meeting. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines & Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
