A Laser & Feelings game about Thirsty Cultists looking for love… in the form of an unfathomable world-ending Horror.

The cult’s leader died in your last attempt to summon the great horror. Can you finish their work and complete the grand ritual?

Who will win the horror’s love? Who will be obliterated? Who will get screwed along the way?

Session 1/1: April 20th, 7:30 pm EST https://gauntlet-hangouts.firebaseapp.com/event-detail/-M1pwu91gpQ6z4Go3dF0

Game available here: https://iterateonwards.itch.io/eldritch-love

Content Warning: Eldritch Love focuses on doomsday cultists actively working to summon a cosmic horror and end the world. They will most likely succeed.

Safety tools: We will be using script change, lines and veils, and an open door policy. We can change these tools to whatever suits the table best. This game follows The Gauntlet’s Inclusivity Policy.

The session is scheduled for around 3 hours and will be recorded and uploaded if everyone agrees to it.
