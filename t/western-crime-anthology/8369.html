This series of games are all in the Western Crime genre, and I intend to frame them all in the same location, albeit separated by time so that we don’t have to constrain ourselves too closely to events in previous games.

As such, while it’s highly recommended that you play all of the sessions for each individual game, you aren’t expected to sign up to all three games.

Seco Creek Vigilance Committee

Seco Creek Vigilance Committee is an Old West RPG about justice, law, revenge, and the discrepancy between what is right and what is good.
