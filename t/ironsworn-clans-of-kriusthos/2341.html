Ironsworn: Clans of Kriusthos

Gauntlet Calendar
Ludovico_Alves June 14, 2019, 2:41pm #1

This event is scheduled to run for 3 hours. It will be recorded assuming all parties agree.

Kriusthos, the City of the Ram, is protected by the courage and ironblood of the youth of its many clans. Grown in proverty, scrapping by in the continental part of the city; they are naturally adventurous and harsh. They wander across the land, doing quests for the disparate clans, trying to learn support - all for the hopes of retiring to leadership of their clan or an august position in the insular part of Kriusthos.

This will be a Guided game with a GM and 4 players.

The game will use the Gauntlet-created setting of the City of Kriusthos.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and safety tools (Script Change and X-Card) will be in use.
