Future Creep: Neon Black
Thursdays, April 2021, 19:30 GMT / 14:30 EST / 11:30 PST
Hello! Here are 5 sessions of Neon Black , a cyberpunk Forged in the Dark game about hidden communities trying to survive and fight back against the oligarchs and the rich that control Prime City. In a climate-ravaged earth, humanity clings to the shadows of isolated corporate city states. Use the tech thrown by the wealthy from their spires to break into their vaults, hack their networks and spray your symbol on their corporate logos. Remind people that life doesn’t need to be this way.

These sessions are being run under a monthly series called Future Creep which aims to run and take a look at different cyberpunk TTRPG’s and how they aim to capture the future that was.

Being able to attend every session is ideal, but not required. You must RVSP to each session you wish to attend.

No prior experience to the system is expected. I will make contact with those signed up a week in advance.

System: Neon Black, a cyberpunk Forged in the Dark game
Duration: 5 three-hour sessions
Breaks: Two 5-minute breaks, at the top of the hour
Venue: Zoom
Recording: Yes, but made public only if consent by all the players
Safety: Lines & Veils, Script Change, Open Door Policy
This series is operating under the Gauntlet’s Community Code of Conduct (which can be found here.)
