The Pitch

World of Gamma is an homage to the 70s and 80s TSR game Gamma World. In this lightweight PbtA game, players assume the roles of mutated animals in a zany post-apocalyptic world fraught with competitive factions, creepy flora, and barely recognizable landmarks.

At-A-Glance

  • System : World of Gamma (a PbtA homage to 1980s Gamma World)
  • The Pitch : Mutant animals adventuring around a familiar post-apocalyptic USA
  • Schedule : Four Tuesdays in January x 2.5-hours/session
  • Breaks : Two 10-minute breaks
  • Tone : Creepy mutations; Exploration; Survival
  • Venue : GM’s Zoom
  • Recording Session : With consent from all players
  • Posting Recording : With consent from all players
  • Attendance : Joining all four sessions is ideal but not required
  • Safety : Lines & Veils, X-Card, Open Table, and any others suggested by the players
