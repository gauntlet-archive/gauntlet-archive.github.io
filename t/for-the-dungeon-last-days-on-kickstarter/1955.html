For the Dungeon! - Last Days on Kickstarter

Hype

My game For the Dungeon! is in its last few days on Kickstarter and wanted to share it with all of you. It’s a game about playing the minions instead of the heroes.

Here are few fun things about the game. It’s based on the principles of improv comedy. Critical failures are one of the most fun parts of the game. You get to play the bad guys; who doesn’t want to be a kobold, mimic, or skeleton. Only $8 digital or $16 for the paperback.

If you want to know more I did an interview with the +1 Forward podcast this site sponsors. You can find it here: https://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/1-forward/for-the-dungeon

Or you can check out the Kickstarter here: https://bit.ly/forthedungeon There are only a few short days left to back so get in on this action while you still can. If you have any questions I’m happy to talk about anything, let’s connect!
