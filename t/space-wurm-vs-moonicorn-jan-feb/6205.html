Space Wurm vs. Moonicorn (Jan/Feb)

Gauntlet Calendar
edige23 December 8, 2020, 4:29am #1

Game Facilitator: Tyler Lominack
I will be running eight sessions of Space Wurm vs. Moonicorn, by Johnstone Metzger, on Wednesdays in Jan and Feb at 8:00 pm (Eastern) on Wednesdays. These will be 3 hours sessions. This will be for 5 players. Attendance in all is ideal, but not required.

Surrounded by their devoted friends and perfidious foes, Space Wurm and Moonicorn wrestle over the fate of the galaxy. Will Space Wurm’s iron coils close around the hearts of man forever? Or will Moonicorn’s beacon shine a light of freedom and joy across the Universe? Battle lines are being drawn and the fate of the Galaxy is in your hands!

We will have a tone and content discussion, we will use Lines and Veils, and the X-Card will be in play. I will be using the Quick Play rules. We will do a world build and character generation in the first session.

Here’s a song to get you in the mood: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=17o1OlroNSE
