Hey peeps
I just want to share with you a small project of mine. It’s a website chronicling the ongoing campaign of Fantasy World my group is playing right now, called Assassins Cheese.
It’s just a brief summary of each session’s fictional events, so it reads like a story. But it also includes each dice roll made, with a link to the relevant move. And a (never 100% up to date) directory of the main Characters and Locations, with pictures taken here and there online.

Comments and questions (and suggestions) are very wellcome.
Enjoy
