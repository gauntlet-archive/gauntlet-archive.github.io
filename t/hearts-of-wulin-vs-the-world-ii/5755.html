Hearts of Wulin vs the World adapts our favorite RPG of romantic Wuxia to play out modern romantic adventures of magical-realism video game-inflected martial arts rock and roll. Think Scott Pilgrim vs the World , the Persona games, maybe Glee or Josie and the Pussycats with more kung fu, add elements of Bollywood, K-Pop, and the Wu-Tang Clan, blend thoroughly, and serve.

We’ll create a group of characters, including their musical talents, and throw them together into a musical romantic wuxia fantasia. Come prepared to describe the soundtrack over your fights, your amazing outfits, and your feelings that can only be properly expressed via music.

We’ll use Safety Tools including Lines & Veils, the X-Card, and Open Door to make sure everyone has maximum fun. This event adheres to The Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct in all other aspects.

You’ll have more fun if you sign up for all 4 sessions, but it’s not required. Note that we’ll have a total of 4 players, one spot is reserved for the player who requested this series.
