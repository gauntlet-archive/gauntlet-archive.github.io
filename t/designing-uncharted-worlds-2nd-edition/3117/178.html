SeanGomes March 4, 2021, 3:24pm #173

Hi all. I know it’s been forever. I’m sorry. Too many issues to go into, some entirely inside my head. It’s been… four months? God.

Firstly, thank you ALL for your continued support of Uncharted Worlds and my efforts in creating UW2. I’m consistently floored by the outpouring of kind words for my humble little offering to the roleplaying scene. Creating a source of fun, of joy, of enjoyment with friends… it means more to me than I can express. I strive to be worthy of that praise.

On to UW2 news. I have an Alpha document ready to go. However, this current thread is rather long in the tooth.

  • On the one hand, a bunch of people are following it, so it makes it easy to get in touch with all of them. Plus there’s a lot of history/development that might be interesting to come folks.
  • On the other hand, a thread this size is daunting, and could put off a lot of folks. Plus a lot of the info is out of date or not relevant anymore, and important stuff could get lost.

So I have a question for you folks: Should I start a fresh, new “Uncharted Worlds 2 Alpha” thread, or should I keep going here (and maybe update the first post)?

Again, sorry for dropping off the face of the earth. I really should have been more communicative, even just to say “nothing to report, having trouble getting out of bed”.
