This session is scheduled for 3 hours. The session will be streamed on YouTube and recorded for Gauntlet Hangouts.
This is a series of one-shots of Never Tell Me the Odds, a game of scoundrels risking it all. We’ll be using the setting created in the Microscope: Hallelujah’s Reach game. Being able to attend each session is not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

YOU ARE A SPACEFARING SCOUNDREL. You’ve blasted, cheated, swindled, double-crossed, charmed, and hyper- jumped your way across HALF THE GALAXY. But now, you’ve made a few bad decisions and gotten into some REAL TROUBLE. The one thing in all the universe that you really care about is in terrible danger, and keeping it safe might require the ULTIMATE SACRIFICE. Are you desperate enough to risk everything you have to SAVE WHAT MATTERS MOST?

We will use the X-card and Script Change during our sessions. We will observe the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy.
