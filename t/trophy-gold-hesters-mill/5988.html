Trophy Gold: Hester’s Mill
Struggling to meet your financial obligations, you have become a treasure-hunter. It’s a dangerous job but… you have pretty much run out of other options. You have heard tell of the ruins of a once-prosperous village in a now-abandoned land: What riches might still lie there? And what dangers?

Trophy Gold is a dark fantasy RPG about desperate treasure-hunters seeking riches in forgotten places. It’s Old School role-playing using a modern lightweight ruleset influenced by story-games.

This game will use safety tools: X-Card, Lines & Veils, and Open Door, and complies with the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
