For the first 72 hours of posting, please only sign up for this if you have not played with me this year. After 8AM Eastern 4/10, anyone may sign up

This is for five May Sunday morning (Eastern) sessions of Hearts of Wulin using the supernatural optional rules. In this PbtA game of wuxia melodrama we will be playing Exorcists, Fox Spirits in Disguise, Cursed Wanderers, and the like. You could end up solving a mystery, falling in love with a ghost, or stopping the plans of a sinister cult.

Hearts of Wulin takes inspiration of movies like Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon and TV Shows like The Untamed. This particular sub-genre draws from films like Bride with White Hair, Painted Skin, and A Chinese Ghost Story. Rules will be taught—attendance at all sessions is strongly encouraged. In session one we will establish tone, create our characters, build the world and get going.

We will be using Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
