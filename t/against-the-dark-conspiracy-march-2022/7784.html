Against the Dark Conspiracy

Against the Dark Conspiracy is a supernatural thriller game in which players take the role of Operators - capable individuals who previously made their living in the world’s overlapping crime and espionage communities, but who now face down the supernatural beings at the heart of the Conspiracy.

NOTE: This will be my first time running or playing AtDC, so only sign up if you’re prepared to ride out the hiccups.

CONTENT WARNING (from Alun Rees): The player characters in this game are not necessarily ‘nice’ people … Horror, Gore, Risk to loved ones, Vampires and their Lore, Tension between player characters, Intimacy between player characters, Verbal or physical abuse between player characters, Drug and alcohol abuse by player characters, Verbal or physical abuse of non-player characters by player characters, Intimacy between player characters and non-player characters.

SESSIONS: There will be 4 sessions in total. Being able to attend each session is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

DURATION: Each session is 3 hours.

NUMBER OF PLAYERS: Three players in addition to the GM.

SAFETY TOOLS IN USE: X-Card, Lines & Veils, Open Door Policy, Stars & Wishes. The game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

VENUE/TECH: Zoom Video, Online Character Keeper, etc. Expect an email one week before the first session, and another shortly before each session.

BREAKS: Two 5-10 minute breaks at the top of each hour.

Sessions will be recorded, but would only be posted with full player consent. Currently the plan is to record only for the GM’s reference.

PRIOR EXPERIENCE REQUIRED: None – rules will be taught in play.
