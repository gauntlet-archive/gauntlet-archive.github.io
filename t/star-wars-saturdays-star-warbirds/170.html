In April, on Saturdays at 9 AM Eastern, I’m running the game Warbirds, hacked a little to fit into Star Wars. What’s Warbirds?
Warbirds is a game about flying and fighting for fame and fortune. Step into the role of a Guild pilot and go head to head in fast-paced aerial and space combat powered by Outrider Studios’ Rapidfire system. Build your fame as a daring ace pilot to receive opportunities to star in HoloVids, sponsor products, feature in graphic netbooks, and gain the perks of sector-wide celebrity.
Games are Saturday mornings at 9 AM Eastern.
