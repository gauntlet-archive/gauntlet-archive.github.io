Remembrace (a playtest)

Gauntlet Calendar
Donogh April 21, 2020, 10:53am #1

A story game of comradeship, betrayal and reconciliation. We will follow a group of comrades in the war of independence turn on each other in civil war.
No knowledge of Irish history required.

This is a playtest of a new game, so expect a little roughness in the mechanics, pacing etc.

Content Warning: will include violence, sectarian conflict, rough justice, and grief.

The session will be recorded on Zoom (for playtest purposes) but will not be made public.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and we will be using Script Change.
