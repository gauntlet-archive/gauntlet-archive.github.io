This is for five Thursday evening (US) sessions of a fantasy steampunk game using Fate. In it you will play part of the crew for a flying opera house moving from city to city for performances. You must put on concerts & plays, deal with hostile audiences, wrangle passengers, fall in love, fight gremlin infestations, see the sights, and take the center stage. It takes inspiration from Andrew Peregrine’s rpg Opera House.

General RSVPs open 7/29.

We will be playing Fate, with stunts and modifications to fit the setting and premise. Rules will be taught. Attendance at all sessions is encouraged. One seat is being reserved for a new Gauntlet player tbd. In session one we will talk safety, make up characters, make some choices about our company’s concert vessel, decide on repertoire, establish relationships, and get to playing.

We will use Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines & Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.
