This one shot game is scheduled for 4 hours. I will email everyone a week ahead to confirm attendance. Sessions will be recorded through Zoom for playtest reference only.

Here Lie Death Gods is a story game that is inspired by the slice of life and mundane magick anime & manga like Omakae Desu and literally any other Shinigami(death gods) format out there. But you do not need to be familiar with either to enjoy this game! This is a loosely Belonging Outside Belonging Game which focuses on a death gods and their day to day life as fetchers of souls. Playbooks center around: ghost that doesn’t want to be fetched, death god assistants, human astral proejects and our characters’ overall relationship w/ regrets or dying. Players choose from a series of prompts to help satisfy a 3 act structure.
