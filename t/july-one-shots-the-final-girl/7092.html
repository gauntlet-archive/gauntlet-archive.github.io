July One-Shots: The Final Girl

When: Thursday 7/1/2021, 8:00 PM EDT

Sessions: 1

Players: 4 (including the facilitator)

Content Warnings: Graphic violence, death, possibly other themes that will be hashed out during game set-up.

The Final Girl is a GM-less horror movie role-playing game meant to emulate slasher flicks or other horror movies where the characters are picked off one-by-one until only one survivor remains to confront the killer. We will create our own movie and find out who will survive to see the end credits.
