Gabe May 9, 2022, 12:10am #1

This is the rock and roll future of yesteryear that never was—and nobody wanted.

It is an intergalactic age of cowboys, outlaws and bandits playing on an interstellar stage.

It is a time of hyper-capitalism and a cutthroat gig economy.

Unreliable trash-heaps carry scrappy underdogs to their next gig,

and corporation freighters lumber across the horizon laden with an empire’s bounty.

The backstreets of every terraformed metropolis brim with a thousand lonely hearts

and a thousand more venal vendettas.

The burnt-out wastelands between bright lights are lawless bat countries

—anachronistic Americana languishing beneath multiple moons.

Madness lies in every direction at every solar hour.

These are the music-fuelled, moon-age daydreams of a rebel space age.

This is session one of a two-shot of Orbital Blues using the Unchained Melody Quickstart . We will make characters and begin the job.

As a system, Orbital Blues lands somewhere between story games and the OSR. Players will portray sad space cowboys who are down on their luck and in need of a job in a way the is reminiscent of Cowboy Bebop, Firefly, The Mandolorian, and maybe even a little Borderlands. It’s hard out here at the edges of the galaxy and things can get pretty lethal.

I will send a reminder 1 week before the session. Rules will be taught. Being excited about sad music a plus, but not required.

CW: Anxiety, Death, Depression, Gambling, Mental Illness and Associated Traumas, Poverty, Smoking, Substance Abuse, Violence

System : Orbital Blues

Duration : 3 Hours

Breaks : 5-10 minutes approximately on the hour

Venue : GM’s Zoom

Recording : No

Safety : Lines & Veils, X-card, and open door policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players. This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
