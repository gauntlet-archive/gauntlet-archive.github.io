Take My Revolution 2-Shots in Oct 11 & Oct 25

Gauntlet Calendar
Nimona September 4, 2022, 9:39am #1

Your school is beautiful but incredibly strange. There are those who call themselves Swordblooms, and they Duel each other in order to impress World’s End and gain the Divine Sword…in doing so, they will create a revolution. They’ll change the world, for the one they love. You are now one of these Swordblooms. The school is alive, and it won’t let you leave. You have no choice. You must duel and take your revolution! Or you can fade away into the shadows…

Take My Revolution is a GMless casual TTRPG heavily inspired by the anime Revolutionary Girl Utena , where students who possibly aren’t actual students duel with swords and hearts in a surreal high school that’s possibly not an actual high school, with mechanics based on the frameworks of Mobile Frame Zero: Firebrands and the use of playing cards from The King is Dead.

Touchstones: Revolutionary Girl Utena, Revue Starlight, Gideon the Ninth, Thirsty Sword Lesbians

Duration : 2 sessions, 3 hours max, with 5 to 10 minutes breaks on the hour. Participation in both sessions are encouraged but not required.

Number of Players: 1 more player needed, with a spot reserved for Thomas Manuel.

Content Warning : PG-13, Teens getting injured, teen angst and emotional turbulence, possible teen romance if desired.

Safety Tools : Lines & Veils, The X-Card, and an Open Door policy. All of which will be explained before the session. It will be run under the Gauntlet Code of Conduct

Venue : Zoom. The session will not be recorded.

Contact : Players will receive an email about a week before the first session to confirm the commitment and receive links to play materials. If you sign-up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
