flatvurm February 17, 2020, 1:02am #5

It’s run by one of our Slack members. The audio is only pulled from the videos submitted for inclusion in the Gauntlet YouTube playlists and the weekly roundup posts on the Gauntlet Blog, which are both publicly available. Consent for public consumption is gathered by the facilitator of each session from all players, and anyone who opts out of public distribution of the video is automatically opted out of the podcast version as well, since the video is never publicly released. Hope that helps!
