Paul_T March 9, 2022, 8:47pm #23

We are still playing and developing/exploring the game.

We’ve got rewritten combat moves (one of our players couldn’t quite keep track of them, somehow, so we simplified them), and now we’re working on Wizardry rules: one of the characters is starting a Grimoire.

Finally, the same PC just established a mercantile enterprise, which is cool. We’re going to see how that develops! (It’s not immediately obvious how often it should be rolled, which feels a bit arbitrary at the moment, so we’ll have to settle on some basic guidelines for that sooner or later.)
