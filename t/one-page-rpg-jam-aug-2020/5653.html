Just a reminder that the rules around organizing sessions are that either you put your game on The Gauntlet calendar and you adhere to the rules of Gauntlet games, or you don’t advertise your sessions on the board at all. And if you want to host a session, you must have a history of participating in Gauntlet sessions to be able to do that. It’s really poorly written in the board rules and people trip over it every other month. You’re free to talk details with interested folks over private messages though but whether you can gauge people’s interest in a public thread is a gray area.
