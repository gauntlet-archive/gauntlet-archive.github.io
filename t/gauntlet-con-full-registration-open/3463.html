RichardRuane October 2, 2019, 2:56am #1

Now anyone, Patreon backer or no, can sign up for Gauntlet Con 2019!

Back us on Patreon: If you choose to back us on Patreon at $2+ or higher (or did so in the past week), you can expedite getting confirmed as an attendee by:

  1. First, logging in to our RSVP site with your Google ID.
  2. Second, leaving a comment on this Patron post.

Back with PayPal: for just a one-time $10 (and ONLY $10!), you can join us. You’ll get an email from us after you buy your admission with directions on next steps. However, if you go ahead and log in to our RSVP site with your Google ID, you’ll be ready to go even faster once that email comes.

But aren’t all the games full?? No! We’ve still got over 90 sessions that still have openings. You can check out this Twitter Thread for some ideas and suggestions, but they only barely crack the surface.
