Looking For Feedback: Get Ready 2 Rock

Game Design
Stentor_Danielson May 6, 2019, 1:39am #1

After a useful playtest on the Gauntlet (as well as Metatopia and Breakout), I’ve produced another iteration of Get Ready 2 Rock, my GMless PbtA game about stupid rock star drama (inspired by things like This Is Spinal Tap, Flight of the Conchords, and VH1 Behind the Music). I’m hoping this version is more streamlined and gets closer to being able to click with how I envision it being able to play.
