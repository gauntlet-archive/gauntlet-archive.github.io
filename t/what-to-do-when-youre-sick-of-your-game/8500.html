What to do when you're sick of your game

Game Design
DeReel November 20, 2022, 4:42pm #1

The DesignDoc duo are speaking in my headset a very good podcast right now. I can compare it to only one other podcast : kF and Eugenie’s podcast on failure states of a game session.
It’s strange, it implies less treaded trails, but to me, it is next level in how I can relate to what they say. Most of the promotional strategies are out the window, I can take what they say in earnest. It’s liberating.
And when you open that door, some fruitful gold nuggets start to fall when you speak. Way to go Hannah and Evan!
