A Great Flexible Earning Opportunity! Add To Part Time Income With Monster Hunting. You’ll Always Start And Stop On Your Time—Because With The #iHunt App, You’re In Charge. Give #iHunt A Try. Be your own boss. Earn extra income. Now with safety features to make sure you get home safe.*

#iHunt is a Fate-based game by Olivia Hill and Filamena Young. You play ordinary people trying to make ends meet by hunting monsters despite terrible pay and conditions, with the unavoidable knowledge that capitalism is the real horror.

I’m going to shift the rules towards the simpler Fate Condensed, and might try out some other house rules. New players are welcome. No rules knowledge is required and all rules will be taught during play.

The tone of the game is kinda darkly humorous action with horror elements. Subject matter includes usual horror stuff - violence, gore, etc - but also poverty, inequality, exploitation, and other social issues. We’ll use lines and veils, the X card and the open door as safety tools, and the game will run under the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.

We’ll be playing over Streamyard, with short breaks on the hour. I’ll send an email about a week before the first session to share play materials, confirm attendance, etc. I’ll ask if it’s okay to record and share the sessions, and will only do so with consent from all players.

Signing up for all sessions is encouraged but not required. Please sign up for all sessions you intend to attend.

Open access time for sign ups is 6:30pm 14 January AEDT.
