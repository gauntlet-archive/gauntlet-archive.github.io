Hello! I am a cadet in Jim Crocker’s awesome Game Facilitator Camp. Each camp participant is running a one-shot for the other cadets to give us hands-on experience creating the calendar event, making the announcement on Slack, and much more. So without further ado:

NOTE THAT THIS EVENT IS LIMITED TO PARTICIPANTS IN JIM CROCKER’S GAME FACILITATOR CAMP

Neon City Overdrive is a game of cyberpunks and the near-future sprawl they run jobs in. It is a game inspired by the attitude, style, and action of Blade Runner, Snow Crash, Neuromancer, and RoboCop. It is a world of gleaming skyscrapers and crowded urban labyrinths where people eke out whatever existence they can claw from the next punk. It’s a grim and gritty world of hard choices, high tech, and limitless danger dressed in chrome and lit in neon.

In this one-shot, we will create characters at the table and then take them on an action-packed job in a near-future San Francisco. Character creation is fast and the rules are intuitive. We’ll be in and out in no time!

Duration : One 3 1/2-hour session
Breaks : Two breaks per session (5-10 mins each)
Venue : GM’s Zoom (link will be shared immediately the event)
Recording : Yes, but made public only with all players’ consent!
Safety : Lines & Veils, Script Change, and Open Door Policy.

This game operates under the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy.

June 15 5:00 PDT: https://gauntlet-hangouts.firebaseapp.com/event-detail/-MbFKYteeCd5LjDcQ6Fi
