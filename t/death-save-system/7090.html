A little death save system you can add to any game: When you have a brush with death, roll a d20. On a 1, you die. On any other result, you scrape by. Then, downgrade your death save die to a d12. Each time you survive, roll a smaller die next time: d20>d12>d10>d8>d6>d4>a coin
