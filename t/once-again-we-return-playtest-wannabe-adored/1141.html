Once Again, We Return is Patrick Knowles’s in-development expansion of Monsterhearts 2, themed to tell stories like Kieron Gillen and Jamie McKelvie’s Image comic, The Wicked + The Divine. From the original WicDiv solicitation: “Every ninety years, twelve gods incarnate as humans. They are loved. They are hated. In two years, they are dead. Just because you’re immortal, doesn’t mean you’re going to live forever.”

All sessions scheduled for a maximum of 4 hours and will be recorded to share if all players consent. General RSVPs begin on March 25, 2019 at 8:00 PM EDT. Attendance at all four sessions is preferred but not mandatory. Session one will incorporate some collaborative setting creation. You must RSVP for each session you wish to attend.

Safety tools in use for this game will include the Open Door and the X-Card, and a content discussion establishing Lines and Veils will be held prior to recording.

If this is the first time you’re playing a Gauntlet Hangout, please review the inclusivity policy.
