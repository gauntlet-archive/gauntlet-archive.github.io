Muir Iasc is free from his imprisonment, and walks amongst mortals once more. Now he feasts on any of Fae blood he can find, and wreaks bloody vengeance on the Fannon’s who exiled him all those years ago.
Now, the Chosen must strike back, to bring peace to Ireland’s Wild West and ensure that this harshest of winters will come to an end.

This is the small town playset ‘Ballhir’ written by Lauren McManamon. This is a continuation of a previous trios of sessions played last year, but if you didn’t play then you’re still more than welcome.

This event is scheduled to run for 3 hours. It will be recorded assuming all parties agree.

There will be 3 sessions in total. Being able to attend each session is ideal but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and safety tools X-Card) will be in use.
