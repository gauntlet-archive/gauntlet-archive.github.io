For nearly a decade, you’ve been robbing rich nobles who pass through your forest. They’ve never managed to catch you or find your hideout. Admittedly, they didn’t try that hard. They had bigger things to worry about. As long as you didn’t get too greedy, you weren’t considered much of a threat.

But the new Sheriff is ruthless, vengeful, and has vowed to wipe out every last outlaw in the forest, and you’ll need all your fighting, seduction, and/or magic skills to save your village.

Yes, this is exactly what it sounds like. Queer Robin Hood, using the PbtA adventure/romance game Thirsty Sword Lesbians.
