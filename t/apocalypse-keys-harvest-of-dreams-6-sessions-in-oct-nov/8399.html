Apocalypse Keys - Harvest of Dreams (6 sessions in Oct / Nov)

Gauntlet Calendar
SabineV5 September 18, 2022, 7:12pm #1

“There is something strange going on in Revere - a town not far away from our headquarter. Seven girls sleep and do not wake, and yet a boy has not been to bed in weeks. The town newspaper has exploded with prophecy, not of the usual kind, but when you read it, you will find slivers of your own story in there. There might not be a Harbinger in Revere, but something is going on, and those prophecies seem to call for you specifically. Go and look, and try not to cause any more harm to reality than you must.”

These will be six sessions in November (at 8:30 pm CEST) of Apocalypse Keys, a game by Rae Nedjadi. Characters in this game are powerful and monstrous, viewed with fear and suspicion by humanity, and they each carry a seed of Darkness; but they are working to prevent an impending apocalypse and save the world. Hellboy is one of the inspirations for these characters.
It is strongly recommended that players sign up for all sessions.

CW: Violence, social isolation, prejudice

System : Apocalypse Keys

Duration : Six 3-hour session (this is the first)

Breaks : 1 every hour (5-10 minutes)

Venue : GM’s Zoom

Recording : No

Safety : Lines & Veils, X-card, and open-door policy. Tools can be changed to suit the players. This game will follow the Gauntlet Community Code of Conduct.
