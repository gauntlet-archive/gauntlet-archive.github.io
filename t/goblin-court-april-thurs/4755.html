Goblin Court by BESW

a TRPG about living your best life in spite of human drama

You are goblins who were brought to live in the royal castle. The Queen & her court know goblins are mystical creatures, but don’t understand what that means—and neither do you; magic is just a goblin thing, you’ve never needed to think about it much.

The Queen expects you to conjure & cast fortunes, to aid her schemes or alleviate her boredom. But be careful; the court is filled with politics, personal problems, human drama, all sorts of confusing stuff for a goblin.

While you work on your own goals, the Queen sets you strange & marvelous tasks rife with hidden political pitfalls. If you help each your Compassion & Cleverness —with maybe a bit of goblin magic— can see you through.

We will be using Zoom for our sessions. I’ll email all players about a week before the first session with the necessary links. Sessions will run up to three hours. We will plan to record, but I will confirm this with the players. The Gauntlet Code of Conduct will be in play and we will use Lines and Veils and the X-Card for safety tools.

If you sign up and your availability changes, please remember to drop from the site and give me a heads up.
