The newest episode of The Farrier’s Bellows is live on the site and in your podcatchers! This week, we look at Jason Morningstar’s WWII historical drama Grey Ranks. We talk about the genre of historical fiction in games and in our lives, and look at how ttrpgs explore childhood, growth and trauma. Enjoy, and let us know what you think!

Thank you to Zack B. for editing and producing.
